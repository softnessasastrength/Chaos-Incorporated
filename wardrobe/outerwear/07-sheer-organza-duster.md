*Soft Light outerwear. Governed by [`../translucent-system.md`](../translucent-system.md). Distinct from [`03-kimono-duster.md`](03-kimono-duster.md) (opaque/print romantic duster).*

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- Same crop rule as the kimono duster: hem **knee to mid-calf max**, never full floor-pool on 64".
- Proportion notes: open front; sleeve 3/4 or pushable — full long organza sleeves are the highest risk for burying the ring.
- Comfort priority: almost weightless; soft organza or chiffon, not stiff crackle; no heavy beading.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: N/A  
- Inseam / length: 34–38" (knee to lower-calf)  
- Waist range / sizing notes: Open front; optional single ribbon tie at empire or natural waist  
- Stretch level: None  
- Fabric weight: Very light, 30–70 gsm  
- Closure type: Open, or single silver-tone clasp / ribbon tie  

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: Soft organza or silk/poly chiffon  
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Airy, cool, barely there over arms  
- Breathability: Excellent  
- Break-in behavior: None  

## Opacity map
- **Entire outer layer:** translucent (veil)  
- **Opacity for the look:** comes entirely from garments underneath — fitted tee, cami, jeans, etc.  
- Never worn as the only layer over underwear for day  

## Colorways
- Cloud Ivory  
- Starlink Emerald (hero veil — **only large Emerald field in the look**)  
- Blush Mauve  
- Lavender Mist  

## Sourcing
- Brand / product line examples: LoveShackFancy organza cover-up, Free People sheer duster, Spell sheer robe/duster, bridal-adjacent soft organza jackets (de-bridalized)  
- Approx. price range: $80–$220  
- Where to buy: brand sites  

## Styling & Pairings
- Pairs well with: lace panel cami + jeans; sheer long-sleeve over tank + shorts; Emerald light look with quiet opaque base  
- Occasion: warm evening, vacation, Soft Light statement layer  
- Shoe pairings: jelly flat, Barely There, Perfect Flat  

## Starlink One Pairing
- Hand/wrist visibility: sleeve **must** clear wrist (3/4 or rolled)  
- Hardware finish: silver-tone clasp only if present  
- Ring echo: Emerald duster is a full-body color filter — no Emerald jelly in the same look  

## Chaos Incorporated Notes
- Aesthetic tie-in: pure soft light as outerwear; structure lives in the outfit underneath.  
- Modifications wanted: crop length non-negotiable; reject floor-length samples.  
- Care instructions: hang, steam, gentle wash or dry clean per fiber.
