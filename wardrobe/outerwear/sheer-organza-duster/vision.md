# The Sheer Organza Duster — Design Vision

*Pure Soft Light as outerwear — Experimental tier. Governed by [`../../translucent-system.md`](../../translucent-system.md). Distinct from [`../kimono-duster/`](../kimono-duster/) (opaque/print romantic duster). See [`../README.md`](../README.md) for its place in the outerwear catalogue and [`../../wardrobe-manifesto.md`](../../wardrobe-manifesto.md) for the doctrine underneath it.*

---

## 1. Name & Philosophy

**The Sheer Organza Duster.**

Where the Kimono Duster is opaque fabric in a floaty, unstructured shape, this piece pushes the same unstructured-duster idea one step further into the translucent system: the entire outer layer is translucent, and unlike the Sheer Overlay Dress, there's no attached underlayer doing the opacity work. Structure and opacity both live entirely in whatever's worn underneath the duster — a fitted tee, a cami, jeans. This piece is pure veil, nothing else, which makes it the cleanest possible expression of "soft light as outerwear" the system has: no compromise toward coverage, because coverage was never this piece's job in the first place.

That clean framing comes with one hard rule, stated as plainly as the Sheer Overlay Dress's own opacity rule: this is never worn as the only layer over underwear for daytime. The duster is an accent on top of a complete outfit, not a load-bearing layer of one.

## 3. Fabric & Feel

- **Fabric: soft organza or silk/poly chiffon**, 30–70 gsm — almost weightless, airy, cool against the arms.
- **Hand-feel:** barely-there, no stiff crackle, no heavy beading.
- **Breathability:** excellent.
- **Break-in behavior:** none.

## 4. Opacity Map

- **Entire outer layer:** translucent — this is a veil, full stop.
- **Opacity for the look:** comes entirely from garments underneath (a fitted tee, a cami, jeans) — this piece contributes zero coverage of its own.
- **Never worn as the only layer over underwear for day** — a hard rule, not a styling suggestion.

## 5. Colorways

- **Starlink Emerald** — the hero veil colorway, and the only large Emerald field permitted in a single look when worn (see Section 6).
- **Blush Mauve.**
- **Lavender Mist.**

## 6. Starlink One Reference & Styling

- **Hand/wrist visibility:** sleeve must clear the wrist (3/4 or rolled) — same mandatory rule as the Kimono Duster.
- **Hardware finish:** silver-tone clasp only, if present.
- **Ring echo:** the Emerald duster functions as a full-body color filter — no Emerald jelly sandal in the same look, the same one-Emerald-lead-per-look rule applied everywhere else in the system, here at its largest single-garment scale next to the Sheer Overlay Dress.
- **Pairing:** lace panel cami plus jeans; sheer long-sleeve base over a tank plus shorts; the Emerald light look with a quiet opaque base underneath.
- **Occasion:** warm evening, vacation, the Soft Light kit's statement layer.
- **Care:** hang, steam, gentle wash or dry clean per fiber.
