# Evening Wrap / Dressy Coat

**Category:** Outerwear  
**Subcategory:** Soft Formal — Arrival Layer  
**Priority:** Medium  
**Status:** Concept  

*Formal arrival shell — replaces moto for Soft Formal. Doctrine: [`../formal-system.md`](../formal-system.md). Distinct from wool winter coat (utility cold) and hardware moto (Chaos night).*

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- **Wrap, capelet, or short dressy coat** to hip or mid-thigh — long opera coats drag and shorten 64" frames unless carefully hemmed.
- Proportion notes: soft structure (shawl collar, clean wrap) not biker hardware; open front or single silver clasp.
- Comfort priority: light enough for indoor carry on the arm; soft lining; not a winter parka pretending to be evening.

## Technical Specs
- Length: hip to mid-thigh (28–36")  
- Closure: wrap tie, single silver-tone clasp, or open  
- Fabric weight: Light–mid formal (crepe, soft wool blend, satin-back crepe)  
- Stretch: 0–3%  

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: Crepe, soft wool-cashmere blend, or dressy satin-back  
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Soft at neck and cuff  
- Breathability: Moderate  
- Break-in behavior: None
