# Soft Formal Suit

**Category:** Outerwear (suit set — blazer + bottom filed here as one system piece)  
**Subcategory:** Soft Formal — Matching Suit  
**Priority:** Medium  
**Status:** Concept  

*Non-dress Soft Formal path. Doctrine: [`../formal-system.md`](../formal-system.md). Distinct from lightweight everyday blazer ([`08-tailored-blazer.md`](08-tailored-blazer.md)) and wide-leg casual trouser.*

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- **Cropped or standard short blazer** (hip bone max) + high-rise trouser or midi skirt keeps vertical line; long boyfriend suit jackets cut 64" frames in half.
- Proportion notes: matching fabric set reads formal; soft shoulder (not football pad); trouser full-length with heel or cropped with precise hem.
- Comfort priority: stretch suiting (2–5% elastane); lined or half-lined jacket; can sit through dinner.

## Technical Specs
- Blazer length: 22–26" (high hip to hip)  
- Trouser: high rise, straight or soft wide-leg with exit strategy (heel assumption 2–2.5")  
- Or skirt: midi pencil or soft A-line matching fabric  
- Stretch: 2–5%  
- Closure: single button silver-tone; trouser zip/hook silver-tone  

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: Stretch wool blend, crepe suiting, or formal ponte set  
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Soft enough for silk cami underneath; no itchy wool at neck  
- Breathability: Moderate  
- Break-in behavior: Minimal  

## Colorways
- Jet Black  
- Midnight Navy  
- Sterling Grey  
- Cloud Ivory (day formal / summer)  

## Sourcing
- Brand / product line examples: Aritzia Babaton matching set, Theory stretch suit, Reformation suit set, MM.LaFleur (if budget), Uniqlo + Theory collab when available  
- Approx. price range: $200–$600 (set)  
- Where to buy: brand sites  

## Styling & Pairings
- Pairs well with: ribbon-tie or lace panel cami (lined), fitted turtleneck (winter), block pump, evening clutch, evening wrap  
- Occasion: black-tie optional, formal work-evening, non-dress wedding guest  
- Shoe pairings: block pump, Anchor Heel  

## Starlink One Pairing
- Hand/wrist visibility: blazer sleeve to wrist bone — pushable  
- Hardware finish: **silver buttons only**  
- Ring echo: none required; quiet suit lets ring lead  

## Chaos Incorporated Notes
- Aesthetic tie-in: hard architecture Soft Formal — soft shell fabric, deliberate structure.  
- Modifications wanted: petite trouser inseam; reject gold crest buttons.  
- Care instructions: dry clean or steam; hang suit together.  
- **Pick suit *or* cocktail dress first** — lifestyle choice, not both day one.
