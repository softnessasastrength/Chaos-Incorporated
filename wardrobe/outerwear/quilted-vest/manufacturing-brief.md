## 1. Product Overview

A hip-length (22–24") quilted vest with a relaxed body and fitted armhole, synthetic or down-alternative fill, zip or snap-front closure with silver-tone hardware. Three colorways share one pattern.

## 6. Starlink One Reference Match

Silver-tone zip pull and snaps only. No further reference-matching requirement — this is a purely functional piece by design (see `vision.md` Section 5).

## 7. Critical Technical Challenges — Open Items for Manufacturer Conversation

1. **Armhole fit at a smaller frame** — confirm the pattern's armhole is graded appropriately rather than linearly scaled down from a standard/larger size, which is the same last-grading-style failure mode flagged in the shoe catalogue's Anchor Heel brief, applied here to an armhole instead of a shoe last.
2. **Fill weight/loft specification** — confirm a specific fill-power or weight-per-area target rather than a generic "light" descriptor, to keep warmth-to-bulk ratio consistent across production runs.
3. **Lining static/cling behavior** — confirm lining fabric performs well layered over the system's typical top fabrics (cotton basics, ribbon-tie camisole silks).

## 8. Requested Next Step

A single fit sample in Sterling Grey, standard/reference size, with explicit armhole fit confirmation, before any multi-colorway production commitment. Given this piece's Medium priority and low overall complexity relative to the rest of the outerwear catalogue, one sample round should be sufficient to move toward production readiness.
