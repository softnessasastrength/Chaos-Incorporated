# The Quilted Vest — Design Vision

*The "no compromise" ring-visibility piece — Medium tier, and the only outerwear item in the system that doesn't require a sleeve-length check at all. See [`../README.md`](../README.md) for its place in the outerwear catalogue and [`../../wardrobe-manifesto.md`](../../wardrobe-manifesto.md) for the governing doctrine.*

---

## 1. Name & Philosophy

**The Quilted Vest.**

Nearly every other piece in this outerwear catalogue carries some version of the same recurring risk assessment: where does the sleeve end, and does it clear the wrist? This piece is the one exception worth calling out explicitly, because it doesn't have a sleeve to hem, roll, or risk getting wrong in the first place. That structural fact makes it the lowest-risk outerwear piece in the entire system for guaranteed ring visibility — not because it was engineered around that goal specifically, but because removing the sleeve removes the entire category of failure mode every other piece has to solve for.

Beyond that functional case, the vest plays a simple, useful role: lightweight warmth without bulk, for the shoulder-season and cool-errand days that don't call for the wool coat's real cold-weather commitment or the denim jacket's structured-but-thin layer.

## 3. Fabric & Feel

- **Fabric: quilted poly shell with synthetic or down-alternative fill** — light-fill, chosen specifically so the piece adds warmth without bulk or stiffness.
- **Hand-feel:** soft quilted exterior, smooth lining.
- **Breathability:** low — an insulating layer by design, not meant for warm-weather wear.

## 5. Starlink One Reference & Styling

- **Hand/wrist visibility:** fully visible by construction — no sleeve at all, the most reliable ring-visible outerwear piece in the entire system. Worth naming directly: this is the piece to reach for on any day the ring needs to stay visible without checking a sleeve-length spec first.
- **Hardware finish:** zip pull and snaps, silver-tone.
- **Ring echo:** none direct — this piece's contribution to the system is purely functional, not aesthetic-motif-based.
- **Pairing:** any `basics/` or `intricate/` top, over leggings or jeans. Treat as either/or with the kimono duster — layering both is too much.
- **Occasion:** shoulder-season, cool-weather errands, a straightforward layering piece.
- **Care:** machine wash cold, tumble dry low or line dry depending on fill type — check the label.
