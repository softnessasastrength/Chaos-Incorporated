# Lightweight Trench

**Category:** Outerwear
**Subcategory:** Structured — Lightweight Trench
**Priority:** Medium
**Status:** Concept

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- A trench's structured shoulder and defined waist belt naturally create the "hard architecture" the manifesto calls for, but full-length trenches (designed for taller frames) hit well past the knee on a 5'4" frame — shorten to just above or at the knee, not mid-calf.
- Proportion notes: sleeve must be hemmed to end at the wrist, the same non-negotiable spec as the cropped denim jacket; the belt is what defines the waist on a shorter torso, so it needs to be functional, not decorative.
- Comfort priority: lightweight cotton-blend gabardine, not the heavier wool-blend trench fabric, so it doesn't feel stiff or overly warm as a three-season piece.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: N/A
- Inseam / length: 34–36" (shortened from a standard 42–44" to knee-max)
- Waist range / sizing notes: Belted, adjustable, functional (not decorative)
- Stretch level: 2–3% elastane in a cotton-blend gabardine
- Fabric weight: Mid, 200–260 gsm
- Closure type: Double-breasted button front plus belt, silver-tone buttons and belt buckle

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: Cotton-poly-elastane gabardine
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Smooth, structured but not stiff, slightly cool to the touch
- Breathability: Moderate
- Break-in behavior: Minimal — the structured shoulder should hold its shape from the first wear

## Styling & Pairings
- Pairs well with: over any tops category — belts naturally over the ribbon-tie camisole or a basics tee for a structured-over-soft look, or over an `intricate/` top for a defined-waist romantic-structured combination
- Occasion: transitional weather, elevated-casual to dressier daytime
- Shoe pairings: ankle boots, loafers, sneakers for a casual break

## Starlink One Pairing
- Hand/wrist visibility: Sleeve must be hemmed to end at the wrist bone — the same mandatory spec as the cropped denim jacket.
- Hardware finish: Buttons and belt buckle, silver-tone throughout.
- Ring echo: Indirect — the belted waist is this piece's own "soft shape held by hard architecture" moment (loose coat body, cinched by a defined belt), the same logic as the ring's heart held by engineered prongs.

## Chaos Incorporated Notes
- Aesthetic tie-in: the most literal garment-scale execution of the ring's own design philosophy in the whole outerwear category.
- Modifications wanted: shorten to knee-max length and hem the sleeve to the wrist — both mandatory, not optional.
- Care instructions: dry clean or gentle machine wash depending on the exact blend — check the label.
