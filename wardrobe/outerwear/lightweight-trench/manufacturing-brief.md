# The Lightweight Trench — Manufacturing Brief

**Document Type:** Technical Specification / Manufacturing Brief for pattern development and factory quoting
**Prepared for:** Structured outerwear manufacturer with trench/tailored-coat capability
**Revision:** 1.0 (Draft — pending manufacturer confirmation of belt hardware sourcing and shoulder structure)

## 1. Product Overview

A knee-max length (34–36") double-breasted trench in lightweight cotton-poly-elastane gabardine, with a functional belted waist, structured shoulder, and sleeve hemmed to the wrist bone. Two colorways share one pattern.

## 2. Pattern & Construction Specification

| Parameter | Target | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Length | 34–36" | Shortened from standard 42–44" — confirm the pattern is genuinely re-proportioned at this length, not just hemmed down from a standard trench block (shoulder-to-hem proportion can look off if only the hem is trimmed) |
| Sleeve | Hemmed to wrist bone | Same gating spec as the cropped denim jacket — confirm against a live fit model |
| Waist | Functional belt, adjustable | Confirm belt loops/keepers hold the belt in a cinched position without slipping loose during normal movement |
| Closure | Double-breasted button front | Silver-tone buttons and belt buckle |

## 3. Fabric Specification

| Parameter | Target | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Fabric | Cotton-poly-elastane gabardine | 200–260 gsm |
| Stretch | 2–3% elastane | Confirm shoulder structure holds shape despite the stretch content — some elastane blends soften structured tailoring more than expected |
| Hand-feel | Smooth, structured but not stiff | Confirm against physical swatch, not spec sheet alone |

## 4. Hardware Specification

Buttons and belt buckle: silver-tone throughout. Standard sourcing, no unusual requirement.

## 6. Starlink One Reference Match

No debossed or chain-link detail. Silver-tone buttons and belt buckle are the full hardware requirement — see `vision.md` Section 5 for the styling-side note that the belted waist itself functions as this piece's structural echo of the ring's own soft-shape/hard-architecture logic (a design framing, not an additional manufacturing spec).

## 7. Critical Technical Challenges — Open Items for Manufacturer Conversation

1. **True re-proportioned pattern at knee-max length** — confirm the shortened trench isn't just a standard-length pattern hemmed down, which can leave shoulder/lapel/pocket placement calibrated for a longer coat.
2. **Sleeve-length fit validation** — confirm against a live fit model, same protocol as the cropped denim jacket.
3. **Shoulder structure retention with stretch fabric** — confirm the 2–3% elastane content doesn't soften the structured shoulder silhouette below an acceptable threshold.
4. **Belt-stay hardware** — confirm belt loops/keepers function reliably during normal movement, not just in a static fit-check pose.

## 8. Requested Next Step

A single fit sample in Cloud Ivory, standard/reference size, with sleeve-length and belt-function confirmed on a live fit model, before any second-colorway production commitment.
