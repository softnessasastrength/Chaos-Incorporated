# Lightweight Tailored Blazer

**Category:** Outerwear  
**Subcategory:** Structured — Lightweight Tailored Blazer  
**Priority:** High  
**Status:** Concept  

*Named backlog gap from the outerwear category — dressier structure without moto heat or trench formality. Soft Heat stack: [`../warm-season-system.md`](../warm-season-system.md).*

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- A **cropped or standard short blazer** (hip-bone max, not long boyfriend) keeps the leg line long; long blazers cut a 5'4" frame in half.
- Proportion notes: slight shoulder structure (soft pad or natural) without linebacker width; single-breasted, not oversized boxy.
- Comfort priority: **unlined or half-lined** summer weight, stretch suiting or soft ponte blazer fabric — a fully canvassed wool blazer fails warm-season and comfort rules for this role.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: N/A  
- Inseam / length: 22–26" body (hits at high hip to hip bone)  
- Waist range / sizing notes: Slight waist suppression or soft darting; true to size or tailoring at sleeve  
- Stretch level: 2–5% elastane preferred  
- Fabric weight: Light–mid, 180–260 gsm summer suiting or ponte  
- Closure type: Single button or open; two-button max  

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: Tropical wool blend, linen-blend suiting with structure, or soft ponte blazer cloth  
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Soft enough for bare arms under sheer sleeve or cami; no scratchy horsehair against neck if unlined  
- Breathability: Good (unlined) to moderate  
- Break-in behavior: Minimal; should drape correctly day one

## Starlink One Pairing
- Hand/wrist visibility: sleeve hemmed to wrist bone or slightly above — **mandatory** (same rule as denim jacket)  
- Hardware finish: silver-tone buttons / crest hardware only — no gold buttons  
- Ring echo: none required; structure recedes so ring and jewelry lead
