# The Wool Wrap / Winter Coat — Manufacturing Brief

**Document Type:** Technical Specification / Manufacturing Brief for pattern development and factory quoting
**Prepared for:** Wool outerwear manufacturer with coat-weight construction capability
**Revision:** 1.0 (Draft — pending manufacturer confirmation of wool sourcing and lining softness)
**Governing doc:** [`vision.md`](vision.md) — read for design rationale; this document is the buildable spec.

---

## 1. Product Overview

A mid-thigh-to-knee length (34–38") wool or wool-blend coat with a wrap or single-button belted waist, soft lining, and a sleeve engineered to clear the wrist when pushed or at rest. Three-to-five colorways depending on the camel/ivory exceptions noted below.

## 2. Pattern & Construction Specification

| Parameter | Target | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Length | 34–38" (mid-thigh to knee) | Confirm on fit sample against 64" reference height — reject standard-length coat patterns that run past knee |
| Waist | Wrap or single-button, soft belt | Confirm belt/wrap mechanism holds a cinched position without slipping |
| Sleeve | Slightly cropped or easy push-cuff | Confirm sleeve accommodates a turtleneck and thin sweater underneath while still clearing the wrist when pushed — this is a layering-dependent fit check, not a single-layer fit check |
| Closure | Wrap tie, toggle, or single/double button | Silver-tone hardware only |

## 3. Fabric & Lining Specification

| Parameter | Target | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Shell | Wool or wool-blend | ~400–600 gsm depending on climate target — confirm which end of this range the intended climate calls for before finalizing fabric order |
| Lining | Soft viscose/cupro or satin | Confirm lining fabric feels cool-smooth against skin, not clingy or static-prone |
| Insulation (optional) | Light recycled fill, only if climate demands | Confirm added insulation doesn't push the silhouette toward puffy/bulky — keeping it slim is a named design requirement, not just a nice-to-have |
| Collar/neck finish | Soft, non-scratchy | Confirm wool at the collar specifically doesn't require a scarf buffer to be comfortable — a scarf is an acceptable styling choice, not a fix for an uncomfortable collar |

## 4. Hardware Specification

Buttons, buckles, toggles: silver or gunmetal only. Standard sourcing, no unusual requirement.

## 5. Colorway Spec Summary

| Colorway | Role | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Jet Black | Core | |
| Midnight Navy | Core | |
| Sterling Grey | Core | |
| Camel | **Off-palette — do not produce** unless recolored to a cool greige within the Sterling Grey family | Flag explicitly to prevent a standard camel wool option from being defaulted into the production run, since camel is an extremely common wool-coat colorway industry-wide |
| Cloud Ivory | Conditional | Only if the specific fabric/finish proves practical against winter dirt/salt exposure — confirm with a wear-test before committing to production |

## 6. Starlink One Reference Match

Silver or gunmetal hardware only. No debossed or chain-link detail — this piece recedes so the ring and other silver jewelry lead once the cuff is clear (see `vision.md` Section 5).

## 7. Critical Technical Challenges — Open Items for Manufacturer Conversation

1. **Layered-sleeve fit validation** — confirm the sleeve clears the wrist when pushed up over a turtleneck-and-sweater combination, not just against bare arms — this is a more complex fit check than the single-layer sleeve validation used elsewhere in outerwear.
2. **Wool weight selection** — confirm 400 vs. 600 gsm (or a point between) based on the intended climate target before fabric ordering.
3. **Camel-default risk** — confirm explicitly that camel is excluded from the production run unless recolored within the Sterling Grey family.
4. **Cloud Ivory practicality** — confirm real-world wear-testing against winter dirt/salt before committing this colorway to a full production run.
5. **Insulation-to-bulk ratio (if added)** — confirm added fill doesn't visibly change the coat's slim silhouette.

## 8. Requested Next Step

A single fit sample in Midnight Navy or Sterling Grey, standard/reference size, with the layered-sleeve fit check performed over a turtleneck and thin sweater, before any multi-colorway production commitment.
