# Soft Heat Shopping Shortlist

*Warm-season capsule. Doctrine: [`warm-season-system.md`](warm-season-system.md). **After Phase 1 Wave A** (and ideally cutoffs/linen shorts). Soft Structure jelly is hero-first only.*

**Hard rules at try-on**
1. Silver hardware on bags and jelly buckles.  
2. One loud Emerald field (clothing *or* jelly, not both large).  
3. Jelly needs real arch footbed — reject flat slab kids’ packs.  
4. Day dress knee-length max on 64".
