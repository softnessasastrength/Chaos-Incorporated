# Fitted Turtleneck

**Category:** Tops  
**Subcategory:** Basics — Fitted Turtleneck Base  
**Priority:** High  
**Status:** Concept  

*Cold-season base layer. Soft Cold: [`../../cold-season-system.md`](../../cold-season-system.md). Extends basics beyond the long-sleeve tee for real cold under coats and blazers.*

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- Fitted (not baggy) turtleneck layers cleanly under coats and blazers without bulk at the waist — same tuck logic as the fitted crew tee.
- Proportion notes: body length to high hip; sleeve to wrist bone (clear for ring when coat is off); turtleneck height moderate — not a face-swallowing funnel.
- Comfort priority: soft fine-gauge knit with stretch; no itchy wool against the neck unless a soft merino with proven next-to-skin rating.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: N/A  
- Inseam / length: Body ~23–25"  
- Waist range / sizing notes: Slim fit; true to size with 4-way stretch preferred  
- Stretch level: 5–15% elastane in cotton, modal, or fine merino blend  
- Fabric weight: Light–mid, 160–220 gsm  
- Closure type: Pullover  

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: Cotton-modal-elastane, fine merino, or soft microfiber knit  
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Soft at neck and cuffs — this is a all-day next-to-skin piece  
- Breathability: Good  
- Break-in behavior: Knits soften; check neck recovery so it doesn’t bag out

- Shoe pairings: boots, flats with socks
