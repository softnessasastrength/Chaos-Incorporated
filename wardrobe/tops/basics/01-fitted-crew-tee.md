# Fitted Crew Tee

**Category:** Tops
**Subcategory:** Basics — Fitted Tee
**Priority:** Must-Have
**Status:** Sourcing

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- A fitted (not tight) crew neck tee is the most universally pairing-flexible top in the system — works tucked into high-rise bottoms without adding bulk at the waist.
- Proportion notes: body length should hit at the high hip when untucked, short enough to tuck cleanly when needed.
- Comfort priority: soft, breathable cotton with a bit of stretch for movement; no stiff seams at the shoulder or neckline.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: N/A
- Inseam / length: Body length ~24" (petite/regular fit, not oversized)
- Waist range / sizing notes: Fitted through the body, true to size chart
- Stretch level: 3–5% elastane in an otherwise 95%+ cotton knit
- Fabric weight: Mid, 180–200 gsm
- Closure type: None — pullover

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: 95% cotton / 5% elastane jersey
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Soft, smooth, slight stretch recovery so it doesn't lose shape after a few wears
- Breathability: Good
- Break-in behavior: Softens with wash; check recovery stretch to ensure it doesn't stretch out at the neckline over time

## Colorways
