# Oversized Boyfriend Tee

**Category:** Tops
**Subcategory:** Basics — Oversized Tee
**Priority:** High
**Status:** Sourcing

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- Oversized fit needs a deliberate proportion counterweight per the manifesto's volume rule — pairs specifically with fitted bottoms (skinny jean, leggings, bike shorts) to avoid an all-over shapeless silhouette.
- Proportion notes: body length should hit mid-hip to upper-thigh — long enough to read as intentionally oversized, not so long it looks like a borrowed dress on a shorter frame.
- Comfort priority: soft, roomy through the body and sleeve — should feel like wearing very little structure at all.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: N/A
- Inseam / length: Body length ~27–28"
- Waist range / sizing notes: Sized up from fitted tee measurements intentionally; drop-shoulder construction
- Stretch level: Minimal, 0–2% — oversized knits don't need stretch to move with the body
- Fabric weight: Mid, 180–220 gsm
- Closure type: None — pullover

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: 100% cotton or cotton-poly blend jersey
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Soft, slightly heavier drape than the fitted tee, relaxed against skin
- Breathability: Good
- Break-in behavior: Softens with washing, may relax slightly at the neckline (expected with drop-shoulder oversized construction)

## Colorways
