# Off-Shoulder Top

**Category:** Tops
**Subcategory:** Intricate — Off-Shoulder
**Priority:** Medium
**Status:** Concept

## Why It Works for Her Frame (63")
- Widens the visual line across the collarbone similar to the cut-out shoulder top, and elongates the neck by exposing it fully — a genuinely useful proportion tool for a shorter frame.
- Proportion notes: fitted body length hitting at the high hip; sleeve should sit securely at the upper arm, not slide down constantly (check for elastic or structured shoulder band).
- Comfort priority: elastic shoulder band needs to be snug enough to stay in place without digging in — this is the most common fit failure point for this style.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: N/A
- Inseam / length: Body length ~22–24"
- Waist range / sizing notes: Fitted through the body, true to size chart
- Stretch level: 6–10% elastane (higher than average, needed for the shoulder band to stay put)
- Fabric weight: Light-mid, 170–200 gsm
- Closure type: None — elasticized shoulder neckline

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: Cotton-elastane or ponte knit
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Soft, smooth, snug at the shoulder band without pinching
- Breathability: Good
- Break-in behavior: Elastic band may loosen slightly over time — check recovery quality before repeat purchase
