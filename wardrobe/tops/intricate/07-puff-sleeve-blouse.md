# Puff-Sleeve Blouse

**Category:** Tops
**Subcategory:** Intricate — Puff Sleeve
**Priority:** High
**Status:** Concept

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- Puff-sleeve volume sits at the shoulder/upper arm, which is proportionally safer volume on a 64" frame than volume at the hip or hem — it doesn't compete with the leg-line math bottoms volume does.
- Proportion notes: body fitted or semi-fitted through the torso so the sleeve is the one volume statement, not one of several; body length hits high hip for tucking; sleeve should stop above the elbow or fit closely from elbow to wrist so it doesn't run long and swallow the forearm.
- Comfort priority: an engineered shoulder seam (elastic or gathered casing, not just loose gathered fabric) is what keeps this from feeling like costume rather than clothing — the manifesto's structural-anchor rule applied directly.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: N/A
- Inseam / length: Body length ~23–24"
- Waist range / sizing notes: Semi-fitted, true to size chart
- Stretch level: 2–4% elastane in a cotton or cotton-blend weave
- Fabric weight: Light, 120–150 gsm
- Closure type: Back zip or button placket

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: Cotton or cotton-poplin with elastane, structured enough to hold the puff shape
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Soft woven body; slightly crisp at the sleeve casing by design (structural), softer through the body
- Breathability: Good
- Break-in behavior: Minimal — sleeve structure shouldn't relax or collapse with wear; if it does within a few wears, it's the wrong construction
