# Lace-Trim Blouse

**Category:** Tops
**Subcategory:** Intricate — Lace Trim
**Priority:** Medium
**Status:** Concept

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- A fitted or semi-fitted blouse silhouette with lace trim at the collar/cuffs keeps the romantic detail concentrated at the face and hands, drawing the eye upward — flattering framing for a shorter frame rather than adding visual weight at the hem.
- Proportion notes: body length should hit at the high hip for tucking into high-rise bottoms.
- Comfort priority: lace trim should be soft (not stiff/scratchy) at points of skin contact — check the collar and cuff lace specifically since those touch skin directly.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: N/A
- Inseam / length: Body length ~24–25"
- Waist range / sizing notes: Semi-fitted, true to size chart
- Stretch level: Minimal (woven cotton or cotton-blend), 0–2% elastane if blended
- Fabric weight: Light, 100–140 gsm
- Closure type: Button-front placket

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: Cotton or cotton-silk blend body with lace trim (nylon or cotton lace)
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Soft woven body, check lace trim softness specifically at contact points
- Breathability: Good
- Break-in behavior: Minimal, woven fabric doesn't relax the way knits do
