# Organza / Chiffon Blouse

**Category:** Tops  
**Subcategory:** Translucent — Semi-Sheer Blouse  
**Priority:** Experimental  
**Status:** Concept  

*Day-safe Soft Light blouse. Governed by [`../../translucent-system.md`](../../translucent-system.md).*

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- Semi-sheer body with a **lined or double-layer bust** keeps romance without lingerie-as-daywear risk.
- Proportion notes: semi-fitted or soft-tuck length to high hip; volume in sleeves (slight puff or bishop) needs a cleaner body so 64" frame isn’t swallowed.
- Comfort priority: washed chiffon or soft organza that moves; avoid stiff crackly organza that scratches at the collar.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: N/A  
- Inseam / length: Body ~23–25"  
- Waist range / sizing notes: Semi-fitted; true to size  
- Stretch level: 0–2% (woven)  
- Fabric weight: Light, 40–80 gsm shell; lining heavier at bust only  
- Closure type: Button-front placket preferred (silver-tone buttons) or keyhole back  

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: Polyester or silk chiffon / soft organza shell; cotton or silky lining at bust panel  
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Fluid, cool; lining soft at bust contact  
- Breathability: Excellent  
- Break-in behavior: Minimal; silk softens slightly  

## Opacity map
- **Bust:** opaque (lined or double layer) — required  
- **Body below bust / back / sleeves:** translucent OK  
- **Worn alone:** day-safe if bust lining holds; still fine over a thin cami for extra security

## Chaos Incorporated Notes
- Aesthetic tie-in: Soft Light’s most wearable “real clothes” piece — structure at the placket and bust, soft light everywhere else.  
- Modifications wanted: reject unlined full-sheer bodies for daywear sourcing.  
- Care instructions: gentle wash or dry clean per fiber; hang dry; steam, don’t iron hot on organza.
