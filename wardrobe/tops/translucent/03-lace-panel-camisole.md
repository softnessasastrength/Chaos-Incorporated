# Lace Panel Camisole

**Category:** Tops  
**Subcategory:** Translucent — Lace Panel Camisole  
**Priority:** Experimental  
**Status:** Concept  

*Extends the ribbon-tie camisole language into true sheer panels. Distinct from [`../intricate/09-ribbon-tie-camisole.md`](../intricate/09-ribbon-tie-camisole.md) (mostly solid). Governed by [`../../translucent-system.md`](../../translucent-system.md).*

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- Adjustable straps tune neckline to a shorter torso; body length hits at or above natural waist for tucking or high-rise pairing.
- Proportion notes: lace panels on sides or upper chest; **bust remains lined/opaque** so the sheer is a designed window, not the whole cup.
- Comfort priority: soft stretch lace (not stiff Guipure edges digging in); lined bust with smooth cup finish.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: N/A  
- Inseam / length: Body ~18–21"  
- Waist range / sizing notes: Fitted; true to size  
- Stretch level: 5–10% in lace/knit zones  
- Fabric weight: Light, 80–140 gsm mixed  
- Closure type: Adjustable straps (ribbon or slider); optional side seam  

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: Stretch lace panels + solid stretch jersey or satin-lined bust  
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Soft lace against skin; smooth lining at bust  
- Breathability: Excellent  
- Break-in behavior: Minimal  

## Opacity map
- **Bust:** opaque / lined — required  
- **Side panels, upper chest insert, or back yoke:** translucent lace OK  
- **Lower body of cami:** solid preferred for day tucking  

## Colorways
- Cloud Ivory  
- Blush Mauve  
- Lavender Mist  
- Starlink Emerald (statement panel color — use carefully)  

## Sourcing
- Brand / product line examples: Free People lace panel cami, Aerie elevated lace cami (check opacity), LoveShackFancy lace cami, Intimately Free People

- Where to buy: brand sites  

## Styling & Pairings
- Pairs well with: under organza duster or open flannel; alone with high-rise jeans/shorts; under sheer long-sleeve base for double Soft Light  
- Occasion: warm weather, date-night layered, Soft Light romantic looks  
- Shoe pairings: Perfect Flat, Barely There, jelly flat  

## Starlink One Pairing
- Hand/wrist visibility: sleeveless — full wrist always  
- Hardware finish: strap hardware silver-tone if metal  
- Ring echo: optional Emerald lace; otherwise quiet so ring leads  

## Chaos Incorporated Notes
- Aesthetic tie-in: Soft Light meets LoveShackFancy DNA — lace as light field, lined bust as architecture.  
- Modifications wanted: reject unlined lace cups for daywear.  
- Care instructions: garment bag, gentle cycle, hang dry.
