# High-Waist Compression Legging

**Category:** Bottoms
**Subcategory:** Leggings / Yoga Pants — Workout / Compression
**Priority:** High
**Status:** Sourcing

## Why It Works for Her Frame (63")
- High compression waistband stays put during movement without needing to be pulled up — important at any height, but especially so where a standard-length waistband may sit at a slightly different point on a shorter torso.
- Proportion notes: confirm petite inseam so the ankle hem doesn't bunch at the shoe during squats/lunges.
- Comfort priority: needs real four-way stretch through hip and knee for full range of motion; the waistband should compress without rolling.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: High (10–11")
- Inseam / length: 25–26" (petite)
- Waist range / sizing notes: Compression fit runs snug by design — size for waist measurement, not aspirational sizing down
- Stretch level: 4-way stretch, 75/25 or 78/22 nylon-elastane or polyester-elastane
- Fabric weight: Mid, 250–300 gsm for structure/compression
- Closure type: None — wide compression waistband, often with an internal pocket

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: Nylon/elastane compression knit
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Firm but not restrictive — should feel supportive at the waist, not tight/pinching
- Breathability: Good — look for moisture-wicking and four-way stretch panels at the knee
- Break-in behavior: N/A, compression fabrics don't relax meaningfully with wear
