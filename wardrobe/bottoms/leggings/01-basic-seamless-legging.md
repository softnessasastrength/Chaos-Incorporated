# Basic Seamless Legging

**Category:** Bottoms
**Subcategory:** Leggings / Yoga Pants — Everyday Base
**Priority:** Must-Have
**Status:** Sourcing

## Why It Works for Her Frame (63")
- The comfort-core foundation of the whole wardrobe — high-rise, full-length, no seams to dig in anywhere on the body.
- Proportion notes: full length should hit at the ankle bone exactly; petite inseam (usually 25–27") avoids bunching at the ankle that a standard length would cause.
- Comfort priority: this is the single most-worn item in the system on low-effort days — zero tolerance for waistband roll-down or seam irritation.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: High (10"+)
- Inseam / length: 25–27" (petite)
- Waist range / sizing notes: True to size chart; check the brand's "high rise" actual measurement, since this varies significantly
- Stretch level: 4-way stretch, 70–80% nylon or polyester / 20–30% elastane
- Fabric weight: Light-to-mid, 200–260 gsm
- Closure type: None — pull-on waistband

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: Nylon/elastane or polyester/elastane blend, seamless knit construction
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Soft, smooth, slightly cool to the touch — should feel like a second skin, not compression gear
- Breathability: Moderate — look for a moisture-wicking finish for all-day wear
- Break-in behavior: N/A — should feel correct from first wear; if the waistband digs in day one, it will not improve
