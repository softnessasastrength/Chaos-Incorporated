# Wide-Leg Yoga Pant

**Category:** Bottoms
**Subcategory:** Leggings / Yoga Pants — Wide Leg / Palazzo
**Priority:** Medium
**Status:** Concept

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- Same "high rise + crop" exit strategy as wide-leg denim, applied to a soft knit — otherwise carries the same risk of swallowing a shorter frame.
- Proportion notes: crop to ankle-length; a full-length wide-leg knit pant will pool on the floor at this height.
- Comfort priority: maximum comfort category — nothing touches the leg below the hip.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: High (10–11")
- Inseam / length: 26–27" (petite, cropped)
- Waist range / sizing notes: True to waist; fitted waistband, loose everywhere else
- Stretch level: 2-way or light 4-way stretch, cotton-modal-elastane blend
- Fabric weight: Light, 180–220 gsm — should drape, not hold rigid shape like denim
- Closure type: None — pull-on waistband, sometimes with a drawstring

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: Cotton/modal/elastane or viscose blend for drape
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Soft, fluid, cool against skin — closer to loungewear than activewear
- Breathability: Excellent
- Break-in behavior: N/A

## Colorways
- Black
- Cream/ecru

## Sourcing
- Brand / product line examples: Beyond Yoga "Weekender Wide Leg," Everlane "Cloud" wide leg pant, Aritzia "TNA" wide-leg lounge pant
- Approx. price range: $70–$120
- Where to buy: BeyondYoga.com, Everlane.com, Aritzia.com

## Styling & Pairings
- Pairs well with: fitted or cropped top
- Occasion: travel, casual lounging that still needs to look put-together
- Shoe pairings: slides, low sandals, sneakers
