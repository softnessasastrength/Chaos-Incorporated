# High-Waist Paperbag Shorts

**Category:** Bottoms
**Subcategory:** Shorts — Paperbag Waist
**Priority:** Experimental
**Status:** Concept

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- The gathered/cinched paperbag waist visually defines the waist very precisely — a strong tool for a shorter torso since it creates a clear horizontal break point at the natural waist.
- Proportion notes: keep the gather subtle rather than voluminous — too much fabric bunched at the waist can add bulk on a smaller frame; length at mid-thigh.
- Comfort priority: belted closure needs to be adjustable and non-digging; test the belt tightness needed to hold the gather vs. what's comfortable seated.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: High (10–11"), with 1–2" of paperbag gather above the natural waistline
- Inseam / length: 4–5"
- Waist range / sizing notes: Belted/tied closure allows some adjustability; check hip fit separately since the waist is adjustable but the hip is not
- Stretch level: 2–4% elastane in a twill or cotton-blend fabric
- Fabric weight: Light-to-mid, 180–220 gsm
- Closure type: Fabric belt or tie closure, side zip

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: Cotton-elastane twill
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Soft-structured, holds the gather shape without stiffness
- Breathability: Good
- Break-in behavior: Minimal — tailored-adjacent piece
