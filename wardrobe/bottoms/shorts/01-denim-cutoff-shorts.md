# Denim Cutoff Shorts

**Category:** Bottoms
**Subcategory:** Shorts — Denim Cutoff
**Priority:** Must-Have
**Status:** Sourcing

## Why It Works for Her Frame (63")
- High rise and a short (not micro) inseam keep the leg line long rather than cutting it short at the widest part of the thigh.
- Proportion notes: inseam should hit at or just above mid-thigh — shorter than that starts to look disproportionate on a shorter leg, longer starts to compete with bootcut/straight jean proportions.
- Comfort priority: raw/frayed hem must be checked for scratchiness at inner thigh — a poorly finished cutoff hem is a real all-day irritant.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: High (10–11")
- Inseam / length: 2–3"
- Waist range / sizing notes: True to waist, same denim sizing logic as the jeans system
- Stretch level: 2–3% elastane
- Fabric weight: 11–12oz
- Closure type: Button-fly, standard 5-pocket

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: 97% cotton / 3% elastane
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Soft-washed exterior; frayed hem should be pre-distressed and softened, not stiff raw-cut denim
- Breathability: Good
- Break-in behavior: Softens further with wash and wear
