# Tailored Bermuda Shorts

**Category:** Bottoms
**Subcategory:** Shorts — Tailored / Bermuda
**Priority:** Medium
**Status:** Concept

## Why It Works for Her Frame (63")
- Longer inseam (knee-length) needs a high rise and a slightly tapered or straight leg to avoid visually shortening the leg — a wide-leg bermuda on a 5'3" frame risks looking boxy.
- Proportion notes: hem should land right at or just above the knee — below the knee starts to shorten the visible leg line significantly at this height.
- Comfort priority: structured fabric (not denim-heavy) for a more polished, breathable warm-weather trouser-short hybrid.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: High (10–11")
- Inseam / length: 7–8"
- Waist range / sizing notes: True to waist, tailored fit through hip
- Stretch level: 2–4% elastane blended into a trouser-weight fabric
- Fabric weight: Light-to-mid, cotton-blend twill or linen-blend
- Closure type: Zip-fly with button/hook waistband closure, belt loops

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: Cotton/elastane twill or linen-cotton blend
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Crisp but not stiff — should feel like a tailored trouser, not casual cotton
- Breathability: Excellent (linen-blend option especially)
- Break-in behavior: Minimal — tailored pieces should fit correctly from first wear
