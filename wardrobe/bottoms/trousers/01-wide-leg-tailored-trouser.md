# Wide-Leg Tailored Trouser

**Category:** Bottoms
**Subcategory:** Trousers — Wide-Leg Tailored
**Priority:** Medium
**Status:** Concept

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- High rise is mandatory here, same logic as the wide-leg jean in `../jeans/jeans-template.md` — it anchors the waist and keeps wide leg volume from reading as swallowing the frame.
- Proportion notes: fitted through hip and waist, wide from thigh down, hemmed to ankle-crop length (never a full break/pool) — the same "exit strategy" the jeans system requires of every wide-leg silhouette.
- Comfort priority: a stretch ponte or suiting blend so this doesn't feel like stiff office-wear; the waistband needs real give even though the piece reads structured.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: High (10–11" front rise)
- Inseam / length: 27–28" (cropped ankle length)
- Waist range / sizing notes: True to waist, tailored through the hip — do not size up for hip room, the wide leg already provides it
- Stretch level: 3–5% elastane in a suiting-weight ponte or twill blend
- Fabric weight: Mid-heavy, structured drape
- Closure type: Hidden hook-and-bar plus zip, or an elastic-back waistband for added give

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: Ponte knit or stretch twill — cotton/poly/elastane or rayon/poly/elastane blend
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Substantial but not stiff — should have real knit give at the waistband against skin
- Breathability: Moderate
- Break-in behavior: Minimal — should sit correctly from the first wear
