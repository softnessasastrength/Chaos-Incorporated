# Skinny Jean

**Category:** Bottoms
**Subcategory:** Jeans — Skinny
**Priority:** Must-Have
**Status:** Sourcing

## Why It Works for Her Frame (63")
- Naturally leg-lengthening on a shorter frame simply by removing fabric volume — no proportion trick required, which makes it one of the lowest-risk silhouettes in the system.
- Proportion notes: hem at or just below the ankle bone. Cropping too short reverses the leg-lengthening effect.
- Comfort priority: the fit sits close to skin at knee and calf for hours, so recovery stretch is the whole game here — this is the one silhouette where comfort lives or dies on fabric recovery, not fit looseness.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: Mid-rise (9–10" front rise) — high-rise skinny can feel restrictive at the natural waist over a full day
- Inseam / length: 27–28"
- Waist range / sizing notes: True to waist; expect a close fit through hip/thigh/knee by design
- Stretch level: 8–12% elastane with a recovery fiber (Lycra/EA blend)
- Fabric weight: 9–11oz
- Closure type: Button-fly, standard 5-pocket

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: 88–91% cotton / 8–12% elastane blend with recovery fiber
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Soft-brushed interior essential — this fabric touches skin continuously at knee/calf, unlike looser cuts
- Breathability: Moderate — higher synthetic content than other styles, worth checking for a moisture-wicking finish if worn in warm weather
- Break-in behavior: Should NOT need breaking in — if it feels tight anywhere on first wear, it will not stretch out enough to fix that; size for immediate comfort

## Colorways
- Black (most versatile, least visible knee-bag over time)
- Mid-blue indigo
- Dark rinse for a dressier register

- Shoe pairings: ankle boots, sneakers, or heels — all sit cleanly against a narrow hem
