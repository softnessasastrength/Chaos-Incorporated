# Boyfriend Jean

**Category:** Bottoms
**Subcategory:** Jeans — Boyfriend
**Priority:** Medium
**Status:** Concept

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- A relaxed-through-hip-and-thigh cut with a slightly slouchy leg — needs the same rise/crop discipline as baggy/wide-leg but in a smaller dose, since the volume is more moderate.
- Proportion notes: cuff the hem once, above the ankle — this is the detail that keeps a boyfriend jean from reading as literally borrowed rather than styled loose on purpose.
- Comfort priority: relaxed seat/thigh similar to mom jean, but with a straighter (less tapered) leg below the knee.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: Mid rise (10" front rise)
- Inseam / length: 27–28" (cuffed)
- Waist range / sizing notes: True to waist; some brands cut this generously through the hip, check size chart against actual hip measurement rather than assuming standard grading
- Stretch level: 1–3% elastane
- Fabric weight: 11–12oz
- Closure type: Button-fly, standard 5-pocket

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: 97–99% cotton / 1–3% elastane
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Classic denim hand, slightly worn-in wash preferred to reinforce the "borrowed, lived-in" aesthetic
- Breathability: Good
- Break-in behavior: Relaxes further with wear — buy true to size rather than sizing up expecting more room

## Colorways
- Medium-worn indigo wash with visible whiskering
