# Baggy / Loose Jean

**Category:** Bottoms
**Subcategory:** Jeans — Baggy / Loose
**Priority:** Experimental
**Status:** Concept

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- The riskiest volume decision in the system — needs an aggressive exit strategy: high rise, cropped/cuffed hem, and a visually defined waist (belt) so the eye reads "waist-in, leg-out" rather than "shapeless."
- Proportion notes: crop at or just above the ankle, cuff once for a clean finish. Full-length baggy on this frame reads as borrowed clothing, not styled volume.
- Comfort priority: the most all-day-comfortable leg possible by design — nothing should touch the leg tightly anywhere below the waistband.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: High (11–12" front rise)
- Inseam / length: 26–27" cropped
- Waist range / sizing notes: Size to the waist, not the hip/thigh — the waistband needs to stay snug even though the leg is loose, or the whole jean slides and requires constant adjusting
- Stretch level: 0–2% elastane
- Fabric weight: 12–13.5oz
- Closure type: Button-fly, high-rise 5-pocket

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: 98–100% cotton, rigid or near-rigid
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Classic indigo denim hand, structured enough to hold shape rather than collapse into a soft heap
- Breathability: Excellent
- Break-in behavior: Rigid denim creases and develops character (whiskering at hip/thigh) with wear — desirable here, reinforces the visible wash/fade the silhouette is often chosen for

## Colorways
- Classic indigo with visible fading/whiskering (does the most aesthetic work in this cut)

## Sourcing
- Brand / product line examples: Levi's "Baggy Dad" (petite sizing available), Abercrombie "Baggy" jean, BDG (Urban Outfitters) "Jaya" baggy jean
- Approx. price range: $70–$110
- Where to buy: Levi's.com, Abercrombie.com, UrbanOutfitters.com

## Styling & Pairings
- Pairs well with: fitted, tucked, or cropped tops only — this is the one silhouette where pairing with another loose piece reads as sloppy rather than intentionally chaotic
- Occasion: casual, streetwear-leaning looks
- Shoe pairings: chunky sneakers or boots, matched in scale to the leg volume

## Starlink One Pairing
- Hand/wrist visibility: N/A — bottoms piece, doesn't affect hand/wrist visibility.
- Hardware finish: Silver-tone, high-rise hardware.
- Ring echo: Indirect — the visible fading/whiskering is this piece's own patina story, the same wear-in-over-varnish philosophy the ring's unprotected silver finish is built on. A material parallel, not a literal motif.

## Chaos Incorporated Notes
- Aesthetic tie-in: the clearest "controlled chaos" test case in bottoms — maximum volume, but only works with maximum waist discipline; a genuine bet, watch how it performs before scaling into more colorways
- Modifications wanted: single cuff at the hem for a cleaner finish than a raw crop
- Care instructions: cold wash inside-out to preserve the wash/fade pattern
