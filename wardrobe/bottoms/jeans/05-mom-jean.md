# Mom Jean

**Category:** Bottoms
**Subcategory:** Jeans — Mom Fit
**Priority:** High
**Status:** Sourcing

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- High rise + tapered ankle is one of the most universally flattering combinations for a shorter torso — the taper narrows the leg line exactly where it's most visible (lower leg) while the roomier hip/thigh stays comfortable.
- Proportion notes: taper should land at or just above the ankle bone; avoid a longer, looser taper that reintroduces the "extra fabric" problem.
- Comfort priority: relaxed through seat and thigh means genuinely all-day comfortable seated wear, closer to legging-comfort than skinny-jean-comfort.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: High (11"+ front rise)
- Inseam / length: 27–28"
- Waist range / sizing notes: True to waist; expect intentional room through hip/thigh
- Stretch level: 2–4% elastane
- Fabric weight: 11–12oz
- Closure type: Button-fly, high-rise 5-pocket, often with a vintage-style stitch detail

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: 96–98% cotton / 2–4% elastane
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Classic vintage-style denim hand — slightly heavier than a modern stretch jean, softened through wash
- Breathability: Good
- Break-in behavior: Softens through the hip/thigh with wear without losing the tapered ankle's shape

## Colorways
- Medium vintage wash (the defining colorway for this silhouette)
- Black for a more modern take
