# Straight-Leg Jean

**Category:** Bottoms
**Subcategory:** Jeans — Straight Leg
**Priority:** Must-Have
**Status:** Sourcing

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- The single most reliable rise/leg combination at 5'4": mid-to-high rise plus a clean, non-tapered, non-flared leg lengthens the line without adding width or shortening the torso.
- Proportion notes: hem should hit at or just above the top of the shoe — ask for a petite inseam or plan to hem, do not buy standard-length and let it pool.
- Comfort priority: this is the "wear it and forget about it" jean — no fit compromise anywhere on the body, all-day mobility at hip/seat.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: Mid-to-high (10–11" front rise)
- Inseam / length: 28–29" (petite/short inseam where offered; otherwise hem from a 30")
- Waist range / sizing notes: True to waist; size for the waist and expect a very slightly loose thigh, which is correct for this silhouette
- Stretch level: 2–3% elastane (97/3 cotton-elastane), stretch concentrated at waist/seat
- Fabric weight: 11–12.5oz
- Closure type: Classic 5-pocket, button-fly or zip-fly, both fine

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: 97% cotton / 3% elastane, rigid-leaning stretch denim
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Brushed interior preferred — raw/unwashed interior denim will feel stiff and slightly scratchy at the inner thigh for the first several wears
- Breathability: Good — cotton-forward blend, no synthetic-heavy fabric that traps heat
- Break-in behavior: Softens noticeably after 3–5 washes; buy true-to-size rather than sizing down expecting stretch-out, since this blend has limited give

## Colorways
- Classic mid-blue indigo (the workhorse — buy this first)
- Black (second colorway once the fit is confirmed)
- Light vintage wash (third, once both above are validated)

## Sourcing
- Brand / product line examples: Levi's 501 or Levi's "Mid-Rise Straight," Madewell "The Perfect Vintage Straight" (petite inseam available), Everlane "'90s Cheeky Straight"
- Approx. price range: $70–$140
- Where to buy: Levi's.com, Madewell.com, Everlane.com — all offer petite/short inseam options, check filters before ordering

## Styling & Pairings
- Pairs well with: fitted or cropped tops (tuck-in works well at this rise), a structured jacket or blazer for the soft/structure balance
- Occasion: everyday — the most versatile single piece in the bottoms system
- Shoe pairings: ankle boots, clean white sneakers, loafers
