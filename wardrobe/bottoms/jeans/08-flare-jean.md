# Flare Jean

**Category:** Bottoms
**Subcategory:** Jeans — Flare
**Priority:** High
**Status:** Sourcing

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- Leg-lengthening when the fitted-thigh-to-flare-from-knee ratio is correct — the fitted portion should end at or just below the knee, giving the flare enough length to lengthen the leg without pooling on the ground.
- Proportion notes: hem must hit exactly at the top of the shoe. Too long reverses the leg-lengthening effect into looking like excess fabric.
- Comfort priority: fitted through hip/thigh similar to a straight leg — comfort profile mirrors straight-leg, not wide-leg.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: High (11" front rise) — flare is highly rise-sensitive, the waist-hip-flare line only reads correctly with a high, well-defined waistband
- Inseam / length: 29–30" (to break exactly at shoe-top; petite length may need slight hem adjustment)
- Waist range / sizing notes: True to waist, expect a fitted thigh
- Stretch level: 2–4% elastane, concentrated through seat/thigh
- Fabric weight: 11–12.5oz
- Closure type: Button-fly, high-rise 5-pocket

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: 96–98% cotton / 2–4% elastane
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Soft-but-structured hand so the flare actually swings when walking rather than hanging stiff — brushed interior at the fitted thigh section
- Breathability: Good
- Break-in behavior: Softens through hip/thigh with wash; flare portion holds its shape independent of break-in

## Colorways
- Dark indigo (most versatile)
- Chocolate brown or black for a '70s-leaning register

## Styling & Pairings

- Occasion: elevated-casual, going-out looks
- Shoe pairings: platform or heeled shoe, needed to keep the hem breaking correctly at shoe-top
