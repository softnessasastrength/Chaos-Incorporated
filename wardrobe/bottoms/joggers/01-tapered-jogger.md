# Tapered Jogger

**Category:** Bottoms
**Subcategory:** Joggers — Tapered
**Priority:** Medium
**Status:** Concept

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- The tapered, cuffed ankle is the same proportion tool as the mom jean's tapered leg — it narrows the visible lower leg on a shorter frame while the body of the pant stays relaxed.
- Proportion notes: inseam should hit right at the ankle bone with the cuff/rib — don't let the tapered portion bunch past the ankle, which reads as too-long on a shorter leg.
- Comfort priority: this is a comfort-core piece by definition — soft fleece or French terry, a drawstring waist that doesn't dig in.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: Mid-to-high (9–10" front rise)
- Inseam / length: 26–27" (petite/short where available)
- Waist range / sizing notes: Drawstring plus elastic waistband
- Stretch level: Minimal fabric give, but the relaxed cut itself allows full mobility
- Fabric weight: Mid, French terry or brushed fleece
- Closure type: Drawstring

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: Cotton/poly French terry blend or brushed fleece
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Soft-brushed interior, substantial exterior hand
- Breathability: Good (terry) to moderate (fleece)
- Break-in behavior: Minimal
