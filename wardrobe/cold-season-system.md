# Cold Season System — Soft Cold

*Capsule doctrine for cool and cold weather in Renn's wardrobe. Mirror of Soft Heat ([`warm-season-system.md`](warm-season-system.md)). Governed by [`wardrobe-manifesto.md`](wardrobe-manifesto.md) and [`color-system.md`](color-system.md).*

**Priority:** Seasonal expansion (High utility once Phase 1 exists)  
**Phase:** Phase 2 seasonal — **source after Phase 1 Must-Haves are fitted**  
**Status:** Concept  

---

## Why This Exists

Phase 1 works year-round in mild climates. Real cold needs:

- A **true coat** (not only denim jacket + cardigan)
- Warm base layers under everything (turtleneck, fleece-lined legging)
- Boots that handle wet/ice without abandoning the system silhouette
- Scarf / gloves that keep silver hardware and ring rules intact

Soft Cold is that map — **Warm Hug in reverse weather**: soft next to skin, structure that holds heat and shape.

| Layer | Role | System pieces |

## Soft + Hard in the cold

| Soft | Hard |
|---|---|
| Fleece interior, brushed knits, scarf drape | Coat structure, boot sole/tread, jean rise |
| Turtleneck next to skin | Defined waist when coat is open |
| Comfort non-negotiable | Sleeve length still clears the **wrist for Starlink One** when coat is off or cuff is pushed |

**Ring rule in cold:** gloves should be **touchscreen-compatible or short enough that the ring can sit above the glove cuff** when she wants it seen; never force gold hardware on glove clasps.

## Cold Comfort Rules

1. **Inseam still first-class** — fleece bulk can eat length; petite inseams required.  
2. **Coat hem** — mid-thigh to knee on 64"; full maxi coats pool and drag.  
3. **One loud color** — Emerald scarf or Emerald turtleneck, not both with Emerald bag.  
4. **Silver hardware only** — coat buttons, zippers, glove snaps.  
5. **Patina over polish** — wool and leather that age are preferred over shiny technical plastic shells (rain boot is the functional exception).  
6. **Do not source Soft Cold ahead of Phase 1** — a coat without base jeans and boots is cosplay.
