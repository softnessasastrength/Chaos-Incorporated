# Ribbon-Tie Sandal

**Category:** Shoes
**Subcategory:** Sandal — Ribbon-Tie
**Priority:** Medium
**Status:** Concept

## Why It Works for Her Frame (63")
- A wrap/ribbon-tie ankle strap adds a small visual anchor at the ankle, which reads as intentional rather than a bare flat sandal disappearing against a shorter leg.
- Proportion notes: keep the ankle wrap slim, not chunky, so it doesn't visually cut the leg short; a low block heel (0.5–1.5") adds some of the "moderate heel worn often" leg-lengthening the shoes category calls for.
- Comfort priority: a cushioned footbed is essential, since a ribbon/strap sandal has minimal structure around the foot itself.

## Technical Specs
- Heel height: 0–1.5" (flat to low block)
- Sizing notes: True to size
- Sole: Leather or rubber outsole with a cushioned footbed
- Closure type: Ribbon-tie or buckle ankle wrap

## Fabric & Feel
- Upper material: Leather or vegan leather straps, with a fabric or grosgrain ribbon-tie option
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Soft strap lining where it touches skin — no raw-edge straps that can rub
- Breathability: Excellent (open sandal)
- Break-in behavior: Leather straps soften within a few wears; if a strap rubs at the same point every time on day one, it's the wrong pair
