# The Jelly Sandal

*The poolside/festival jelly sandal — High tier, built on a confirmed favorite rather than a named gap or an untested bet. Read [`vision.md`](vision.md) first, then [`manufacturing-brief.md`](manufacturing-brief.md) for the factory spec.*

The first shoe in the catalogue built from injection-molded PVC/TPU rather than leather, suede, canvas, or jute — and the first piece anywhere in this system to openly break, rather than bend, the "materials chosen to patina" rule. The translucent Starlink Emerald colorway is the resolution: the shoe doesn't wear the ring's color, it's made of it. See [`../README.md`](../README.md) for its place in the full shoe catalogue.

## Status
Concept stage — full vision and manufacturing brief drafted, nothing sampled or physically confirmed yet.
