# The Wrap Ballet Flat

*The dance-inspired ribbon-wrap flat — Medium tier, the most literal balletic reference in the catalogue. Read [`vision.md`](vision.md) first, then [`manufacturing-brief.md`](manufacturing-brief.md) for the Shenzhen-ready factory spec.*

The deliberate inverse of [`../perfect-flat/`](../perfect-flat/): that piece hides its engineering (concealed wedge, raised vamp); this one has almost none to hide, on purpose. Shares ribbon-tie construction language with [`../barely-there-sandal/`](../barely-there-sandal/) and [`../espadrille-wedge/`](../espadrille-wedge/) but reads as a dance-costume reference rather than a resort one. See [`../README.md`](../README.md) for its place in the full shoe catalogue.

## Status
Concept stage — full vision and manufacturing brief drafted, nothing sampled or physically confirmed yet. See `manufacturing-brief.md` Section 7 for the open items a factory needs to weigh in on before tooling — most notably whether the ribbon anchor loops hold up under daily tie/untie cycles, the highest-handling detail in the whole catalogue — and Section 8 for the recommended first prototype scope (aniline nappa, one size, both wrap configurations tested).
