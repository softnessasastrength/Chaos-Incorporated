# The Perfect Flat

*The everyday flat — the Must-Have "neutral variable" shoe the bottoms system pairs against. Read [`vision.md`](vision.md) first, then [`manufacturing-brief.md`](manufacturing-brief.md) for the Shenzhen-ready factory spec.*

Not to be confused with a plain ballet flat — the loafer-height vamp and fully concealed 0.5" internal wedge are what make this a hybrid, not a standard flat. See [`../README.md`](../README.md) for its place in the full shoe catalogue.

## Status
Concept stage — full vision and manufacturing brief drafted, nothing sampled or physically confirmed yet. See `manufacturing-brief.md` Section 8 for the open items a factory needs to weigh in on before tooling — most notably confirming the wedge stays fully concealed with no visible seam at the outsole edge — and Section 9 for the recommended first prototype scope (primary slip-on only, one size, one colorway).
