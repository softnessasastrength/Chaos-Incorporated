# Jelly Ankle-Strap Sandal

**Category:** Shoes  
**Subcategory:** Jelly Line — Ankle-Strap Sandal  
**Priority:** Experimental  
**Status:** Concept  

*Part of Soft Structure — see [`README.md`](README.md). Warm-weather open shoe that can get wet; not a substitute for the Barely There ribbon sandal’s romantic leather register.*

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- An ankle strap adds a small visual anchor at the ankle — same leg-line logic as the Barely There’s Everyday Strap version — so a flat open shoe doesn’t “disappear” and shorten the leg.
- Proportion notes: **slim** straps only (toe + midfoot + ankle); multi-wide sporty jelly straps cut the leg and fight the system’s romantic-casual DNA.
- Comfort priority: open construction breathes better than the closed jelly flat; strap edges must be soft-finished or the ankle rubs within an hour.

## Technical Specs
- Heel height: 0–1" (flat to low block)
- Sizing notes: true to size; adjustable ankle strap is required (not fixed molded loop only) so one last works across slight instep variation
- Sole: flexible TPU with light tread
- Footbed: molded anatomical + arch; removable insert preferred
- Closure type: adjustable ankle strap with silver-tone buckle (chain-link face preferred)

## Fabric & Feel
- Upper material: soft translucent TPU/PVC straps + same-material sole unit, or molded one-piece with defined strap geometry
- Hand-feel / skin feel: soft strap contact at ankle and toe; no sharp mold flash
- Breathability: excellent
- Break-in behavior: straps should not need “stretching in” — TPU that only fits after blistering is the wrong product
