# Everyday Jelly Flat

**Category:** Shoes  
**Subcategory:** Jelly Line — Everyday Flat (Ballet / Mary-Jane Hybrid)  
**Priority:** Experimental  
**Status:** Concept  

*Hero of the Soft Structure jelly line. Read [`README.md`](README.md) first for shared construction rules. Source this silhouette first — Emerald + Cloud Ivory only — before any other jelly SKU.*

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- A near-flat keeps the same “neutral leg-line variable” role as the Perfect Flat, so it can drop into warm-weather looks without rewriting bottoms math.
- Proportion notes: rounded toe, slim mary-jane strap (not a wide kid strap), optional **0.5" hidden internal wedge** so it still reads flat while taking pressure off the forefoot — same proportion logic as [`../01-everyday-ballet-flat.md`](../01-everyday-ballet-flat.md).
- Comfort priority: molded arch footbed is mandatory; classic uncontoured jelly flats fail the manifesto’s comfort-is-the-aesthetic rule immediately.

## Technical Specs
- Heel height: 0–0.5" (external flat silhouette; hidden wedge optional)
- Sizing notes: true to size; confirm last width on sample — jellies that run narrow blister at the little toe
- Sole: flexible TPU outsole with light tread (wet sidewalk capable, not ice-rated)
- Footbed: molded anatomical bed + real arch; removable memory-foam insert preferred
- Closure type: single slim strap across the vamp (buckle or molded tab); open back or closed heel cup both acceptable if heel cup is soft-edged

## Fabric & Feel
- Upper material: soft translucent TPU/PVC per line construction rules
- Hand-feel / skin feel: cool-to-the-touch, flexible, no sharp molded edges at the strap or heel counter
- Breathability: moderate (closed jelly traps heat more than a sandal — this is a trade for water tolerance)
- Break-in behavior: should be wearable day one; if it digs at the strap on wear one, wrong size or wrong mold
