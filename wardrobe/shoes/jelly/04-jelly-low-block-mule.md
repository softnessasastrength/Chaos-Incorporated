*Part of Soft Structure — see [`README.md`](README.md). The only intentional heel in the jelly line — moderate, not going-out. Distinct from [`../07-statement-mule.md`](../07-statement-mule.md) (Phase 3 sculptural mule) and [`../06-block-heel-pump.md`](../06-block-heel-pump.md).*

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- A **1.0–1.5" block** adds usable leg-lengthening for shorts and cropped jeans without the rare-occasion commitment of the 2.5–3" statement heels.
- Proportion notes: open back mule keeps the ankle free (no strap cutting the leg); block base must be stable enough to walk a full day, not a spike under translucent plastic.
- Comfort priority: mules that slip force toe-clenching — vamp fit and footbed grip (slight texture or removable insert with soft top) are required so the shoe stays on without clawing.

## Technical Specs
- Heel height: 1.0–1.5" stable block
- Sizing notes: true to size; check vamp depth carefully — too shallow and the mule fails; too deep and it looks clumsy on a smaller foot
- Sole: TPU outsole with block heel unit same material or bonded stable core
- Footbed: molded anatomical + arch; non-slip top surface or insert
- Closure type: slip-on mule (no ankle strap)

## Fabric & Feel
- Upper material: soft translucent TPU/PVC vamp, same Soft Structure hand-feel as the flat
- Hand-feel / skin feel: soft vamp edge at the topline — no cutting ridge across the instep
- Breathability: good (open back)
- Break-in behavior: should walk cleanly day one; heel slip of a few mm is normal, full lift-off is a fit fail
