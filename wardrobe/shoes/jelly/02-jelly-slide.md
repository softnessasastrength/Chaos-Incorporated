## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- A single wide-but-not-chunky vamp strap keeps the foot visually continuous without an ankle strap cutting the leg — cleaner proportion under shorts and cropped hems than a multi-strap jelly.
- Proportion notes: low profile, close to the foot; avoid oversized pool-slide bulk that shortens the leg.
- Comfort priority: this is the water-tolerant cousin of the yoga/recovery shoe — footbed comfort is the product; the strap only has to hold without rubbing.

## Technical Specs
- Heel height: 0–0.25" (true flat)
- Sizing notes: true to size with slight forefoot room; slides that are short in the toe box fail first
- Sole: flexible TPU, light tread, rinse-friendly
- Footbed: full molded anatomical bed + arch; removable insert preferred for rinse
- Closure type: slip-on; single vamp strap (molded integral or bonded)

## Fabric & Feel
- Upper material: soft translucent TPU/PVC per line rules
- Hand-feel / skin feel: smooth strap underside where it crosses the foot — no flash ridges
- Breathability: good (open sides)
- Break-in behavior: immediate comfort required; no “softens in a week” excuse for a slide
