## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- A closed clog keeps the foot dry in light rain without the visual and thermal bulk of the full waterproof Chelsea rain boot — useful when the day is wet but not cold.
- Proportion notes: keep the clog silhouette relatively slim (no oversized nurse-clog volume); a slight rocker sole helps walk without looking orthopedic.
- Comfort priority: closed jelly traps heat — breathable perforations or a removable insert that can dry between wears is required if this is meant for multi-hour wear.

## Technical Specs
- Heel height: 0.5–1.0" (slight lift, stable)
- Sizing notes: true to size with room for a thin sock if desired; clog lasts that run short fail first
- Sole: deeper tread than the flat/slide — light rain and wet pavement, **not** ice or deep snow
- Footbed: molded anatomical + arch; removable insert mandatory for dry-out between wears
- Closure type: slip-on clog or elastic-side chelsea mule hybrid

## Fabric & Feel
- Upper material: soft but slightly thicker TPU/PVC than the open silhouettes (more structure, still flexible)
- Hand-feel / skin feel: soft collar at the opening; no rigid rim cutting the ankle
- Breathability: fair — the weakest in the jelly line by design (closed + waterproof-adjacent)
- Break-in behavior: should flex at the forefoot without cracking; if the upper is rigid out of box, wrong compound
