# Clean Sneaker

**Category:** Shoes
**Subcategory:** Sneaker
**Priority:** Must-Have
**Status:** Sourcing

## Why It Works for Her Frame (63")
- A low-profile silhouette (not a chunky platform sneaker) keeps proportions clean — a bulky sole can shorten the leg line the same way excess bottoms volume can, so this stays a "quiet" piece rather than a statement one.
- Proportion notes: low-top silhouette preferred over high-top, which can visually cut the ankle and shorten the leg on a shorter frame.
- Comfort priority: this is the highest-mileage shoe in the rotation — cushioning and break-in behavior matter more here than in any other shoe category.

## Technical Specs
- Sole height: ~1" (low-profile)
- Sizing notes: true to size for leather styles; canvas styles sometimes run large, check reviews
- Sole: rubber outsole, cushioned midsole
- Closure type: lace-up

## Fabric & Feel
- Upper material: leather or canvas
- Hand-feel / skin feel: soft interior lining, padded collar to prevent heel rub
- Breathability: good, especially canvas versions
- Break-in behavior: leather styles need 1–2 wears to soften at the crease; canvas is comfortable from first wear
