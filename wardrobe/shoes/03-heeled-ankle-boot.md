# Heeled Ankle Boot

**Category:** Shoes
**Subcategory:** Ankle Boot
**Priority:** High
**Status:** Sourcing

## Why It Works for Her Frame (63")
- This is the shoe the jeans system's fit math explicitly assumes: `bottoms/jeans/jeans-template.md` names a heeled ankle boot as the pairing that completes wide-leg, flare, and bootcut jeans' leg-lengthening effect — without it, those silhouettes lose part of their intended proportion.
- Proportion notes: a 1.5–2" block or stacked heel is the "moderate heel worn often" the shoes README calls for — more usable leg-lengthening across a whole week than a high heel worn rarely.
- Comfort priority: cushioned footbed and a stable (not stiletto-thin) heel base matter more at this height, since this shoe is meant to be worn often, not just for a single event.

## Technical Specs
- Heel height: 1.5–2", block or stacked
- Sizing notes: true to size; break-in note applies at the ankle cuff, not the toe box
- Sole: rubber outsole with a stable heel base
- Closure type: side zip

## Fabric & Feel
- Upper material: leather or suede
- Hand-feel / skin feel: cushioned footbed despite the heel, soft ankle cuff lining
- Breathability: moderate (leather), better with a suede upper
- Break-in behavior: leather/suede softens at the ankle cuff within a few wears; heel stability shouldn't need "breaking in" — if it feels wobbly on day one, it's the wrong pair
