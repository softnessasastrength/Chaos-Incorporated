# Everyday Ballet Flat

**Category:** Shoes
**Subcategory:** Everyday Flat
**Priority:** Must-Have
**Status:** Sourcing

## Why It Works for Her Frame (63")
- A flat adds zero height, which means it's the "neutral" variable in the leg-lengthening math established across the bottoms system — it works best paired with silhouettes that don't depend on a heel to complete their proportion (straight-leg, skinny, cropped-straight, mom jean's tapered ankle).
- Proportion notes: a rounded or almond toe reads softer and more in-scale with a smaller frame than a very long pointed toe, which can look oversized on a shorter foot.
- Comfort priority: this is a walked-in-constantly piece — footbed cushioning is not optional.

## Technical Specs
- Heel height: 0–0.5"
- Sizing notes: true to size; check if the brand runs narrow, since flats have the least room to compensate for a fit error
- Sole: rubber outsole, leather or cushioned foam insole
- Closure type: slip-on, or a single strap/buckle

## Fabric & Feel
- Upper material: soft leather or high-quality leather-alternative
- Hand-feel / skin feel: soft interior lining, no rigid seams across the toe box
- Breathability: Good (leather), moderate (synthetic alternatives)
- Break-in behavior: quality leather flats soften at the vamp within the first few wears; if it's still painful after that, it's the wrong size
