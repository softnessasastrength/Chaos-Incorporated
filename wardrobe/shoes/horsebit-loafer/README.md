# The Horsebit Loafer

*The structured loafer — High tier, and the one everyday-to-elevated shoe where the Starlink One reference is meant to be seen, not found. Read [`vision.md`](vision.md) first, then [`manufacturing-brief.md`](manufacturing-brief.md) for the Shenzhen-ready factory spec.*

Distinct from [`../perfect-flat/`](../perfect-flat/)'s soft, hidden-engineering ballet-loafer hybrid — this is a genuinely structured loafer with a mini stacked heel and a custom chain-link horsebit replacing the traditional snaffle. See [`../README.md`](../README.md) for its place in the full shoe catalogue.

## Status
Concept stage — full vision and manufacturing brief drafted, nothing sampled or physically confirmed yet. See `manufacturing-brief.md` Section 7 for the open items a factory needs to weigh in on before tooling — most notably sourcing the custom chain-link horsebit hardware — and Section 8 for the recommended first prototype scope (aniline nappa, one size, hardware approved independently before the full sample).
