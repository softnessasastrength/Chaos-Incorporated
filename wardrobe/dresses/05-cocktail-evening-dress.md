## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- **Knee or just-above-knee** cocktail length is the most leg-lengthening formal silhouette at 64" when paired with a moderate heel.
- Proportion notes: fitted bodice, defined waist; neckline open enough for formal jewelry but not Soft Light unlined risk — bust lined.
- Comfort priority: stretch lining, sit-test through dinner; no rigid bones that dig after one hour unless soft spiral boning.
