# Ring Statement Dress

**Category:** Dresses
**Subcategory:** Statement — Ring-Forward Fitted Dress
**Priority:** Experimental (Phase 3 Statement)
**Status:** Concept

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- This is the single piece in the entire system built explicitly to showcase the ring — a fitted or semi-fitted silhouette with a 3/4 or cropped sleeve that keeps the wrist and hand visible through normal movement (reaching, resting, gesturing), rather than as an accident of length like other pieces.
- Proportion notes: knee-length max, fitted through the bodice and waist with a defined waistline — a shorter, fitted length is more leg-lengthening than any longer or looser silhouette at this height. This is the one piece in the system allowed to prioritize "ring-showcase" fit logic above the usual comfort-first hierarchy, precisely because it's a rare-occasion piece, not a weekly-wear one.
- Comfort priority: still non-negotiable per the manifesto — fitted does not mean restrictive. Stretch fabric and a structured-but-flexible bodice (boning or a built-in liner, not raw compression) are required.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: N/A
- Inseam / length: 33–35" (knee)
- Waist range / sizing notes: Fitted with a structured bodice
- Stretch level: 6–10% elastane in a crepe or ponte-like woven
- Fabric weight: Mid, 180–220 gsm
- Closure type: Back zip or hidden side zip

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: Crepe, scuba knit, or structured ponte with real stretch and recovery
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Smooth, substantial but not stiff, structured through the bodice, softer through the skirt
- Breathability: Moderate
- Break-in behavior: Minimal — this is a fitted piece meant to fit correctly from the first wear, not relax into shape

## Colorways

- Midnight Navy

## Styling & Pairings
- Pairs well with: worn alone as the full statement — the heeled ankle boot or block-heel pump, the layering bracelet on the same wrist as the ring, minimal other accessories so nothing competes with the dress-and-ring pairing
- Occasion: the system's true "going out" piece — dinner, event, date night, anywhere the ring is meant to be the visible centerpiece of the whole look
- Shoe pairings: block heel pump, heeled ankle boot

## Starlink One Pairing
- Hand/wrist visibility: Sleeve is specced 3/4-length or shorter specifically so the ring stays visible through natural arm movement, not just when the arm is still — this is the one item in the system where ring visibility is the primary design brief, not a secondary check.
- Hardware finish: Back zip, silver-tone.
- Ring echo: Direct and total in the Starlink Emerald colorway — the closest a single garment gets to being a literal extension of the ring's own color.
