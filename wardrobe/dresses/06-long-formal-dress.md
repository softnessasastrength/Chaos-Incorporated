## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- Floor length on 64" **only works with a plan**: heel height fixed first (2–3" block), then hem so the front clears the floor when walking — no pooling.
- Proportion notes: column or softly flared from hip; **defined waist**; avoid wide ballgown skirts that erase height.
- Comfort priority: light enough to wear 4+ hours; soft lining; slit or ease for walking if column is narrow.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: N/A  
- Inseam / length: floor with **intended heel** — tailor after shoes owned  
- Waist range / sizing notes: Fitted bodice; alterations budget assumed  
- Stretch level: 2–8%
