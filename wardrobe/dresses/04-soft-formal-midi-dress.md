# Soft Formal Midi Dress

**Category:** Dresses  
**Subcategory:** Soft Formal — Workhorse Midi / Tea Length  
**Priority:** High (within Soft Formal only — still not wardrobe Must-Have)  
**Status:** Concept  

*Primary re-wearable formal piece. Doctrine: [`../formal-system.md`](../formal-system.md). Distinct from soft day dress (casual romantic) and ring statement dress (Emerald showcase going-out).*

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- **Tea to midi length** (below knee, above ankle) is the formal length that lengthens a 64" frame without floor-pool risk.
- Proportion notes: fitted or semi-fitted bodice, **defined waist**, soft A-line or gentle column skirt — not ballgown volume.
- Comfort priority: stretch crepe or soft satin with lining; can sit through a ceremony and meal; sleeves 3/4, cap, or sleeveless with optional wrap.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: N/A (dress)  
- Inseam / length: 38–42" typical tea/midi on 64" with heel — verify on body with intended shoe  
- Waist range / sizing notes: Defined waist; true to size with stretch  
- Stretch level: 4–8% elastane preferred  
- Fabric weight: Mid, 160–240 gsm crepe/satin  
- Closure type: Invisible zip  

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: Stretch crepe, soft satin, or quality ponte formal — re-wearable, not crackly costume  
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Smooth lining; no scratchy seams at underarm  
- Breathability: Moderate  
- Break-in behavior: Minimal — must fit correctly first wear

## Styling & Pairings
- Pairs well with: evening wrap, wool coat (cold), block pump or Anchor Heel, evening clutch, layering silver  
- Occasion: wedding guest, daytime formal, black-tie optional when midi is accepted  
- Shoe pairings: block heel pump, Anchor Heel, dressy Barely There slide (warm)
