# Soft Day Dress

**Category:** Dresses  
**Subcategory:** Everyday — Soft Romantic Day Dress  
**Priority:** High  
**Status:** Concept  

*The warm-season everyday dress — not the ring statement dress and not the sheer overlay. Soft Heat: [`../warm-season-system.md`](../warm-season-system.md). Distinct from [`01-ring-statement-dress.md`](01-ring-statement-dress.md) and [`02-sheer-overlay-dress.md`](02-sheer-overlay-dress.md).*

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- Knee or just-above-knee length lengthens the leg more than mid-calf floaty maxi; defined waist (smocked, seamed, or tie) is required so volume has an exit strategy.
- Proportion notes: fitted or semi-fitted bodice + soft skirt (A-line, gentle gather, or light tier) — not empire-only shapeless, not bodycon rare-occasion.
- Comfort priority: soft cotton, linen-blend, or stretch-crepe that can be worn all day; pockets preferred; no stiff lining that turns into a sauna.

## Technical Specs
- Rise: N/A (dress)  
- Inseam / length: 33–36" (above-knee to knee)  
- Waist range / sizing notes: Defined waist; true to size with smocked back ease ideal  
- Stretch level: 2–8% depending on fabric  
- Fabric weight: Light–mid, 100–180 gsm  
- Closure type: Side zip, back zip, or pull-on smocked  

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: Cotton poplin, linen-cotton, soft rayon crepe, or smocked cotton  
- Hand-feel / skin feel: Soft, breathable, lived-in friendly (patina-over-polish fabric, not precious silk only)  
- Breathability: Excellent to good  
- Break-in behavior: Cotton/linen soften with wash

## Styling & Pairings
- Pairs well with: lightweight blazer, cropped denim jacket, Barely There sandal, Perfect Flat, jelly flat (Ivory), hair ribbon, everyday or statement bag  
- Occasion: everyday warm weather, brunch, travel day, casual dinner  
- Shoe pairings: Perfect Flat, Barely There, Anchor Heel, jelly flat/slide  

## Starlink One Pairing
- Hand/wrist visibility: sleeveless, cap, or 3/4 — never full long sleeve that covers the wrist in the default version  
- Hardware finish: zip silver-tone if visible  
- Ring echo: quiet base colors let ring lead; Emerald solid is a direct color echo — skip Emerald jelly and heart pendant in that colorway  

## Chaos Incorporated Notes
