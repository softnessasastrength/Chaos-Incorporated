## Fit checklist (every try-on)

1. **Inseam / hem** — no pooling; crop or petite before “I’ll hem it later” as the plan.  
2. **Rise** — mid/high preferred for 64" unless Experimental low-rise is intentional.  
3. **Wrist** — sleeves end at or before wrist bone for ring visibility.  
4. **Hardware** — bag/jewelry/buttons: silver, gunmetal, pewter — **walk away from gold defaults**.  
5. **Comfort after 20 minutes** — sit, walk, reach; if it fails now, it fails the manifesto.  
6. **Color** — pull from the 9-color palette in [`color-system.md`](color-system.md).
