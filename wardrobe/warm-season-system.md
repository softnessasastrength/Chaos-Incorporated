# Warm Season System — Soft Heat

*Capsule doctrine for warm weather in Renn's wardrobe. Pulls Soft Structure (jelly), Soft Light (translucent), and existing warm-weather separates into one rotation map. Governed by [`wardrobe-manifesto.md`](wardrobe-manifesto.md) and [`color-system.md`](color-system.md).*

**Priority:** Seasonal expansion (High utility once Phase 1 exists; individual pieces stay at their own tiers)  
**Phase:** Phase 2 seasonal — **source after Phase 1 Must-Haves are fitted**  
**Status:** Concept  

---

## Why This Exists

Phase 1 is built for *every day, any season*. Warm months need a different rotation density:

- Open shoes that can get wet (jelly) vs romantic dry sandals (Barely There)
- Translucent layers that breathe (Soft Light) vs opaque knits
- Shorts / skirts / day dresses instead of only jeans + leggings
- Hair ribbons and scarves as heat-friendly accessories

Without a map, warm-weather shopping becomes random. Soft Heat is the map.

## The Three Registers

| Register | Doctrine | Role in heat |
|---|---|---|
| **Foundation** | Phase 1 Must-Haves | Still the base — ivory tank, straight jean, cutoffs, crossbody, ring |
| **Soft Structure** | [`shoes/jelly/README.md`](shoes/jelly/README.md) | Wipeable, water-tolerant, translucent color at the foot |
| **Soft Light** | [`translucent-system.md`](translucent-system.md) | Sheer/semi-sheer layers; opacity map always on |

Use **one Experimental register as the personality lead** per look when possible:

- Soft Structure day → jelly is loud, clothing quieter  
- Soft Light day → sheer field is loud, shoes quieter (or ivory jelly only)  
- Emerald light → one Emerald translucent field, no Emerald jelly

## Heat + Comfort Rules

1. **Fabric weight first** — prefer 40–140 gsm shells in heat; save ponte/moto for AC or night.  
2. **Wrist still clear** — heat is not an excuse for long sleeves burying Starlink One.  
3. **Silver hardware only** — including bag and blazer buttons.  
4. **One loud color field** — especially Emerald.  
5. **Underlayers for Soft Light are not optional** — opacity map from translucent system applies in 95° weather too.  
6. **Jelly is seasonal, not heirloom** — replace when sticky; don’t force patina logic onto TPU.

---

## What Soft Heat Does Not Cover

- Full swimwear system (intimate-adjacent; out of manifesto outerwear scope for now)  
- Performance athletic kit beyond existing bike/running shorts and compression leggings  
- Winter outerwear (trench, rain boot, fleece-lined legging remain the cold map)
