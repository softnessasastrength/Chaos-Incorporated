## Why This Combination Works
- **Manifesto check:** an all-soft outfit like this is the one case the manifesto explicitly flags as needing a structured counterpart somewhere — here that job falls to the crossbody bag's structured exterior and the sweatshirt's crew construction, since there's no jacket in this look. A day this outfit gets worn with the cropped denim jacket layered on top pushes it fully back on-brand.
- **Color system:** Deep Forest Green legging is the system's most direct non-jewelry color echo of Starlink Emerald — this is the look built specifically to prove that pairing works in practice, not just on paper in `color-system.md`.
- **Comfort:** every piece in this look is a documented Must-Have chosen for all-day wear (seamless waistband, sneaker, structured-but-soft bag) — no fit compromise anywhere, matching the manifesto's non-negotiable comfort rule.

## Starlink One
- The Deep Forest Green legging and the ring's emerald are a tonal echo, not a color clash — same family, different piece, worn on different parts of the body.
- Sweatshirt sleeve should still end at or before the wrist (per the fitted-crew-tee and cardigan precedent in this system) so the ring and the layering bracelet stay visible even in a fully casual look.
