# Full Look: Romantic Filter

**Occasion:** Brunch, warm-weather daytime, soft romantic-casual.  
**Phase:** 2 Experimental Soft Light — dry-weather version uses Barely There; wet/play version can swap jelly ankle-strap.  
**Registers:** palette-tinted translucency as a romantic filter, not lingerie daywear.

## The Pieces
- **Top:** [`../tops/translucent/02-organza-chiffon-blouse.md`](../tops/translucent/02-organza-chiffon-blouse.md) — Blush Mauve (lined bust)
- **Bottom:** [`../bottoms/jeans/10-cropped-straight-jean.md`](../bottoms/jeans/10-cropped-straight-jean.md) — classic mid-blue or light wash
- **Shoes:** [`../shoes/04-ribbon-tie-sandal.md`](../shoes/04-ribbon-tie-sandal.md) — Cloud Ivory or Blush Mauve (Everyday Strap)
- **Hair:** [`../accessories/04-hair-ribbons-scrunchies.md`](../accessories/04-hair-ribbons-scrunchies.md) — Blush Mauve ribbon
- **Jewelry:** [`../accessories/02-layering-necklace-bracelet-set.md`](../accessories/02-layering-necklace-bracelet-set.md) + Starlink One
- **Optional layer:** [`../outerwear/01-cropped-denim-jacket.md`](../outerwear/01-cropped-denim-jacket.md) — black or mid wash if temperature drops (structure half)

## Why This Combination Works
- **Opacity map:** blouse bust lining does the hard work; jeans are fully opaque; no accidental sheer seat.
- **Romantic without costume:** Blush Mauve filter over denim is LoveShackFancy × Target DNA — soft light, real bottom.
- **Soft + hard:** sheer blouse + hard denim + optional denim jacket; ribbon hair echoes Barely There ribbon language.
- **Color system:** Blush Mauve + denim indigo + silver — Emerald only on the ring, so the stone leads.

## Starlink One
- Blouse cuff clears wrist; sleeveless if 3/4; ring visible through motion.
- Quiet palette on the hand-adjacent zone (no Periwinkle sleeve flooding the wrist).
- Hair ribbon and sandal ribbon are motif cousins of packaging/romantic DNA, not competitors to the ring’s chain-link.

## Translucent System Check
- Day-safe Soft Light per [`../translucent-system.md`](../translucent-system.md).
- No Emerald jelly in this look — keeps Emerald exclusive to Starlink One.
