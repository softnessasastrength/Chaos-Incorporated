# Full Look: Soft Structure Translucent Day

**Occasion:** Warm-weather daytime — errands that might include sun, pool-adjacent, vacation day.  
**Phase:** 2–3 Experimental (Soft Light + Soft Structure) — not a Phase 1 shopping target.  
**Registers:** soft light over solid base + material-translucent footwear; one Emerald field only.

## The Pieces
- **Base top:** [`../tops/basics/03-ribbed-tank-top.md`](../tops/basics/03-ribbed-tank-top.md) — Cloud Ivory (opaque architecture)
- **Translucent layer:** [`../tops/translucent/01-sheer-long-sleeve-base.md`](../tops/translucent/01-sheer-long-sleeve-base.md) — Cloud Ivory sheer over the tank
- **Bottom:** [`../bottoms/shorts/05-linen-drawstring-shorts.md`](../bottoms/shorts/05-linen-drawstring-shorts.md) — Cloud Ivory or Sage
- **Shoes:** [`../shoes/jelly/01-everyday-jelly-flat.md`](../shoes/jelly/01-everyday-jelly-flat.md) — **translucent Starlink Emerald** (the single loud Emerald field)
- **Bag:** [`../accessories/01-everyday-crossbody-bag.md`](../accessories/01-everyday-crossbody-bag.md) — Jet Black or Sterling Grey (quiet)
- **Jewelry:** [`../accessories/02-layering-necklace-bracelet-set.md`](../accessories/02-layering-necklace-bracelet-set.md) — same wrist as Starlink One

## Why This Combination Works
- **Opacity map satisfied:** tank handles bust/torso opacity; sheer sleeve is pure light field; shorts are solid.
- **One loud Emerald:** jelly flat only — top stays Ivory so the ring and the shoe share emerald without fighting a third green field.
- **Soft + hard:** soft sheer + soft linen, hard architecture from tank structure, bag structure, and jelly footbed/hardware.
- **64" proportion:** fitted layers, short hem, flat shoe that doesn’t rewrite leg math.

## Starlink One
- Sheer sleeve clears the wrist per base-layer spec — ring fully visible.
- Layering bracelet matches silver-family jelly buckle language.
- Ring is the metal + stone detail; shoe is the color echo at the ground — two different registers, no competition at the hand.

## Translucent System Check
- Follows [`../translucent-system.md`](../translucent-system.md) opacity map and one-loud-element rule.
- Pairs Soft Light clothing with Soft Structure footwear without requiring every piece to be new Experimental SKUs (tank + shorts already exist).
