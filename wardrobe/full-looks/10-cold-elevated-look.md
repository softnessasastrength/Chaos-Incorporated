# Full Look: Cold Elevated Day

**Occasion:** Cold elevated — lunch, travel day, winter dinner-casual.  
**Phase:** 2 Soft Cold  
**Registers:** soft dress or tailored bottom + wool coat structure + heel or knee boot.

## The Pieces
- **Dress or separates:**
  - **Option A:** [`../dresses/03-soft-day-dress.md`](../dresses/03-soft-day-dress.md) — Cloud Ivory or Blush Mauve, with turtleneck only if dress neckline allows layering  
  - **Option B:** [`../tops/basics/07-fitted-turtleneck.md`](../tops/basics/07-fitted-turtleneck.md) — Cloud Ivory + [`../bottoms/trousers/02-straight-leg-ponte-trouser.md`](../bottoms/trousers/02-straight-leg-ponte-trouser.md) — Jet Black  
- **Shell:** [`../outerwear/09-wool-wrap-coat.md`](../outerwear/09-wool-wrap-coat.md) — Sterling Grey or Midnight Navy  
- **Shoes:** [`../shoes/anchor-heel/`](../shoes/anchor-heel/) — Jet Black (dry) **or** [`../shoes/05-knee-high-boot.md`](../shoes/05-knee-high-boot.md)  
- **Bag:** [`../accessories/07-statement-shoulder-bag.md`](../accessories/07-statement-shoulder-bag.md) — Jet Black or Cloud Ivory  
- **Scarf (optional):** [`../accessories/05-everyday-scarf.md`](../accessories/05-everyday-scarf.md) — quiet neutral  
- **Jewelry:** Starlink One + layering set; skip heart pendant if anything Emerald is already in the stack  

## Why This Combination Works
- **Soft + hard:** soft day dress or soft turtleneck under structured wool coat — manifesto at cold-weather scale.
- **Elevated without moto heat:** coat replaces hardware moto for winter; silver hardware still system-true.
- **Distinct from look 09:** heel/knee boot and statement bag instead of rain boot and crossbody.

## Starlink One
- Coat sleeve push-up mandatory for ring visibility indoors and at table.
- Quiet palette so emerald stone leads.
