## Why This Combination Works

- **No double-denim conflict.** The cropped denim jacket's own notes flag that denim-on-denim needs deliberate tonal contrast — this look sidesteps that question entirely by pairing the jacket with a legging instead of a jean, so there's no wash-matching decision to get wrong.
- **Color system check:** Cloud Ivory (tank) + Deep Forest Green (legging, a direct Starlink Emerald echo) + classic indigo (jacket, a neutral in this system) + Cloud Ivory (sneaker) — every piece is on-palette per `../color-system.md`, and the legging is the one piece doing deliberate color storytelling relative to the ring.
- **Ring visibility, checked against every piece:**
  - Tank is sleeveless — wrist and hand fully visible.
  - Jacket sleeve is speced to hit exactly at the wrist bone (see the jacket's own Starlink One Pairing notes) — it does not cover the ring even when worn.
  - Bracelet sits just above the wrist bone, sized deliberately not to collide with the ring.
  - Net result: the ring is visible whether the jacket is on or off, which is the actual test of whether a look "pairs cleanly with the ring" rather than just not actively hiding it.
- **Frame/proportion check:** legging has zero volume risk, jacket is cropped to avoid shortening the torso, sneaker is low-profile so it doesn't compete with the jacket's cropped proportion at the other end of the silhouette.
- **Hardware check:** jacket buttons/rivets and bracelet are both specified silver-tone/sterling silver — no gold anywhere in the look, per the closed hardware rule.
