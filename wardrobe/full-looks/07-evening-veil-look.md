# Full Look: Evening Veil

**Occasion:** Soft evening, dinner, date night that isn’t full moto-and-pump statement.  
**Phase:** 2–3 Experimental Soft Light  
**Registers:** sheer as evening filter; grounding neutrals; ring leads.

## The Pieces
- **Translucent top:** [`../tops/translucent/01-sheer-long-sleeve-base.md`](../tops/translucent/01-sheer-long-sleeve-base.md) — Sterling Grey or Lavender Mist *or* Jet Black sheer if available under palette rules  
- **Opaque base:** [`../tops/basics/03-ribbed-tank-top.md`](../tops/basics/03-ribbed-tank-top.md) — Jet Black or Cloud Ivory (bust/torso opacity)  
- **Bottom:** [`../bottoms/trousers/02-straight-leg-ponte-trouser.md`](../bottoms/trousers/02-straight-leg-ponte-trouser.md) — Jet Black or Midnight Navy  
- **Shoes:** [`../shoes/anchor-heel/`](../shoes/anchor-heel/) Everyday or Dress Anchor — Jet Black **or** [`../shoes/06-block-heel-pump.md`](../shoes/06-block-heel-pump.md) if true going-out  
- **Bag:** [`../accessories/07-statement-shoulder-bag.md`](../accessories/07-statement-shoulder-bag.md) — Jet Black  
- **Jewelry:** Starlink One + [`../accessories/02-layering-necklace-bracelet-set.md`](../accessories/02-layering-necklace-bracelet-set.md); optional [`../accessories/06-heart-emerald-pendant-necklace.md`](../accessories/06-heart-emerald-pendant-necklace.md) **only if** sheer top is *not* Emerald and bag is *not* Emerald  

## Why This Combination Works
- **Opacity map:** tank + ponte handle all required opaque zones; sheer is sleeves/shoulders light only.
- **Evening without costume:** black/grey structure + soft veil reads intentional, not lingerie-as-outfit.
- **Soft + hard:** soft mesh, hard trouser line and heel architecture.
- **Distinct from look 03:** no moto, no ring statement dress — quieter Soft Light evening lane.

## Starlink One
- Sheer sleeve clears wrist; ring is the saturated accent on a quiet field.
- Pendant optional and secondary — never with Emerald sheer + Emerald bag.
