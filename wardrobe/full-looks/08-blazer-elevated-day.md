# Full Look: Blazer Elevated Day

**Occasion:** Elevated daytime — travel, lunch, “put together” warm or mild weather.  
**Phase:** 2 High (once blazer + soft day dress exist)  
**Registers:** soft romantic dress + hard tailored architecture; ring visible; no competing Emerald fields.

## Why This Combination Works
- **Soft + hard at outfit scale:** soft day dress (or organza blouse) under tailored blazer — manifesto rule executed as a full look.
- **64" proportion:** cropped/hip-length blazer + knee dress keeps vertical line; Anchor Heel optional for more leg length.
- **Color system:** quiet neutrals; ring is the emerald; bag hardware silver-only.
- **Warm season utility:** unlined/light blazer avoids moto-level heat while still reading intentional.

## Starlink One
- Dress sleeve and blazer sleeve both clear the wrist — double-check layering so blazer cuff doesn’t hide the ring.
- No heart-emerald pendant needed; ring has the hand.
