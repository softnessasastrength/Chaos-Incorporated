# Full Look: Cold Errand Day

**Occasion:** Cold everyday — errands, transit, low-effort but warm.  
**Phase:** 2 Soft Cold (after Phase 1 bases + boots exist)  
**Registers:** soft next to skin, hard weather shell, function-first footwear.

## The Pieces
- **Base:** [`../tops/basics/07-fitted-turtleneck.md`](../tops/basics/07-fitted-turtleneck.md) — Sterling Grey or Jet Black  
- **Bottom:** [`../bottoms/jeans/01-straight-leg-jean.md`](../bottoms/jeans/01-straight-leg-jean.md) **or** [`../bottoms/leggings/05-fleece-lined-legging.md`](../bottoms/leggings/05-fleece-lined-legging.md) — Jet Black  
- **Shell:** [`../outerwear/09-wool-wrap-coat.md`](../outerwear/09-wool-wrap-coat.md) — Midnight Navy or Jet Black  
- **Shoes:** [`../shoes/08-rain-snow-boot.md`](../shoes/08-rain-snow-boot.md) — Jet Black (wet/ice) **or** [`../shoes/03-heeled-ankle-boot.md`](../shoes/03-heeled-ankle-boot.md) — Jet Black (dry cold)  
- **Scarf:** [`../accessories/05-everyday-scarf.md`](../accessories/05-everyday-scarf.md) — Cloud Ivory or Blush Mauve (soft face)  
- **Gloves (optional):** [`../accessories/08-soft-gloves.md`](../accessories/08-soft-gloves.md) — Jet Black  
- **Bag:** [`../accessories/01-everyday-crossbody-bag.md`](../accessories/01-everyday-crossbody-bag.md) — Jet Black  
- **Jewelry:** Starlink One + [`../accessories/02-layering-necklace-bracelet-set.md`](../accessories/02-layering-necklace-bracelet-set.md) when cuffs are clear  

## Why This Combination Works
- **Soft Cold stack:** soft turtleneck + hard coat + weather-appropriate boot.
- **64" proportion:** knee/mid-thigh coat + straight jean keeps leg line; rain boot is the deliberate flat exception for safety.
- **Color system:** navy/black grounding; soft scarf is the romantic note; ring is the emerald.
- **One loud field:** no Emerald coat + Emerald scarf.

## Starlink One
- Push coat and turtleneck cuffs to clear the wrist when outdoors and safe to show the ring.
- Gloves: short cuff mode so ring can sit above, or accept covered hands in deep cold.
