# Full Look: Emerald Light

**Occasion:** Elevated vacation daytime, soft statement, “I dressed with intention” warm weather.  
**Phase:** 3-adjacent Experimental — Soft Light hero color.  
**Registers:** one large Starlink Emerald translucent field; everything else recedes.

## The Pieces
- **Base:** [`../tops/basics/01-fitted-crew-tee.md`](../tops/basics/01-fitted-crew-tee.md) — Cloud Ivory (or [`../tops/basics/03-ribbed-tank-top.md`](../tops/basics/03-ribbed-tank-top.md) Ivory if heat requires)
- **Emerald translucent field (pick one only):**
  - **Option A — duster:** [`../outerwear/07-sheer-organza-duster.md`](../outerwear/07-sheer-organza-duster.md) — translucent Starlink Emerald  
  - **Option B — sheer sleeve:** [`../tops/translucent/01-sheer-long-sleeve-base.md`](../tops/translucent/01-sheer-long-sleeve-base.md) — translucent Starlink Emerald  
  - **Option C — overlay dress:** [`../dresses/02-sheer-overlay-dress.md`](../dresses/02-sheer-overlay-dress.md) — Emerald sheer over Ivory under *(replaces top + bottom)*
- **Bottom (if not Option C):** [`../bottoms/shorts/01-denim-cutoff-shorts.md`](../bottoms/shorts/01-denim-cutoff-shorts.md) or [`../bottoms/skirts/02-translucent-midi-skirt.md`](../bottoms/skirts/02-translucent-midi-skirt.md) — **Cloud Ivory only** if skirt (no second colored sheer)
- **Shoes:** [`../shoes/01-everyday-ballet-flat.md`](../shoes/01-everyday-ballet-flat.md) — Cloud Ivory **or** [`../shoes/04-ribbon-tie-sandal.md`](../shoes/04-ribbon-tie-sandal.md) — Cloud Ivory  
  - **Do not** wear Emerald jelly in this look — Emerald is already the clothing field
- **Jewelry:** Starlink One + optional [`../accessories/02-layering-necklace-bracelet-set.md`](../accessories/02-layering-necklace-bracelet-set.md) only — **no** heart-emerald pendant (duplicate stone motif)

## Why This Combination Works
- **One loud Emerald field:** the translucent garment is the only large green; ring is small saturated green at the hand — tonal echo, not competition.
- **Ivory architecture underneath:** opacity map held by solid base/underlayer; Emerald is light filter, not unlined risk.
- **Controlled chaos:** high personality, still engineered (base + length + wrist clearance).
- **Soft Structure rhyme without double Emerald:** quiet shoes so Soft Light owns the statement.

## Starlink One
- This is the clothing-scale version of “the wardrobe is designed around the ring” without requiring the opaque ring statement dress.
- Wrist clear on all options; duster sleeve 3/4 mandatory.
- Pendant stays home — two emerald hearts/stones in one look would crowd the ring.
