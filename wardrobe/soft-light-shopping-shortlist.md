# Soft Light Shopping Shortlist

*Sheer / translucent register. Doctrine: [`translucent-system.md`](translucent-system.md). **Do not start until Phase 1 Wave A is owned and fitted** — see [`phase-1-shopping-shortlist.md`](phase-1-shopping-shortlist.md).*

**Hard rules at try-on**
1. Opacity map: bust + seat covered (lining or solid base).  
2. Sleeve clears wrist for Starlink One.  
3. One large Emerald field max per look.  
4. Soft mesh/chiffon next to skin — reject scratchy costume nylon.
