## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- Compact structured shoulder or short-strap bag sits at high hip / waist — not a giant tote that overwhelms a smaller frame.
- Proportion notes: small-to-medium footprint; short shoulder strap or top handle + optional crossbody chain so it doesn’t swing at mid-thigh.
- Comfort priority: padded or soft strap edge; weight under ~1.5 lb empty; still usable for phone, card case, keys, lip balm — not a pure clutch that forces a second bag.

## Technical Specs
- Strap: short shoulder and/or silver-tone chain strap (detachable ideal)  
- Size: compact structured (roughly mini to small — not tote)  
- Closure type: flap magnetic, turn-lock, or zip — secure enough for dinner/transit  

## Fabric & Feel
- Material: smooth leather, soft pebbled leather, or quality vegan leather with structure  
- Hand-feel / skin feel: soft hand on strap; structured body that holds shape  
- Breathability: N/A  
- Break-in behavior: leather softens at fold points; structure of body should hold
