# Soft Gloves

**Category:** Accessories  
**Subcategory:** Cold Weather — Soft Gloves  
**Priority:** Medium  
**Status:** Concept  

*Soft Cold hand layer. Soft Cold: [`../cold-season-system.md`](../cold-season-system.md). Must not break silver-hardware or ring-visibility rules.*

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- Gloves are proportion-neutral if slim; avoid oversized ski gauntlets that swallow the wrist and forearm visually.
- Proportion notes: wrist length that can sit **under** a coat cuff cleanly; not so long they bunch under every sleeve.
- Comfort priority: soft knit or fine leather with soft lining; touchscreen fingertips preferred for real life.

## Technical Specs
- Length: wrist to low forearm max  
- Fit: snug through fingers, not tight enough to restrict circulation  
- Closure type: none, or silver-tone snap only  

## Fabric & Feel
- Fabric composition: soft merino/cashmere-blend knit, or fine leather with soft lining; technical softshell only if still “warm hug” hand-feel  
- Hand-feel / skin feel: soft interior; no rough seams at fingertips  
- Breathability: moderate  
- Break-in behavior: leather softens; knit should be right day one  

## Colorways
- Jet Black  
- Sterling Grey  
- Cloud Ivory  
- Midnight Navy  
- Blush Mauve (soft romantic cold)  

## Sourcing
- Brand / product line examples: Uniqlo heattech gloves, Everlane cashmere glove (if offered), Coach leather glove (silver hardware only), smartwool/merino glove lines  
- Approx. price range: $20–$120  
- Where to buy: brand sites, department stores  

## Styling & Pairings
- Pairs well with: wool coat, trench, rain/snow boot days, scarf  
- Occasion: cold outdoor transit  
- Shoe pairings: N/A  

## Starlink One Pairing
- Hand/wrist visibility: **design for two modes** — (1) ring worn above glove cuff when glove is short, or (2) ring off / under glove when full coverage needed; never require gold clasps  
- Hardware finish: silver-tone only if any metal  
- Ring echo: none; gloves are utility soft  

## Chaos Incorporated Notes
- Aesthetic tie-in: soft materials, no competing jewelry metal.  
- Modifications wanted: touchscreen pads; reject gold zipper pulls common on fashion gloves.  
- Care instructions: knit — gentle wash; leather — conditioner.
