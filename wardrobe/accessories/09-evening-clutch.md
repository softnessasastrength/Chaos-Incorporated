# Evening Clutch

**Category:** Accessories  
**Subcategory:** Soft Formal — Evening Clutch / Mini  
**Priority:** Medium  
**Status:** Concept  

*Formal bag scale. Doctrine: [`../formal-system.md`](../formal-system.md). Distinct from everyday crossbody and statement shoulder bag (both larger / day-elevated).*

## Why It Works for Her Frame (64")
- Small clutch or minaudière stays in-scale; oversized evening totes read casual and overwhelm a smaller frame in formal photos.
- Proportion notes: handheld or short silver chain that sits high — not long crossbody drop at hip for black-tie rooms.
- Comfort priority: holds phone + card + lip + key; under ~0.8 lb empty; optional short chain so hands can be free briefly.

## Technical Specs
- Size: compact clutch / mini envelope  
- Strap: none, or detachable short silver-tone chain  
- Closure: magnetic flap, zip, or frame clasp — silver-tone  

## Fabric & Feel
- Material: smooth leather, satin, or structured crepe to match formal fabrics  
- Hand-feel / skin feel: soft edges; no sharp hardware against arm  
- Break-in behavior: minimal  

## Colorways
- Jet Black  
- Cloud Ivory  
- Sterling Grey / pewter metallic (cool silver family only — **no gold**)  
- Starlink Emerald (only with quiet dress)  
- Blush Mauve  

## Sourcing
- Brand / product line examples: Coach clutch silver hardware, Cuyana mini pouch elevated, Dagne Dover slim (if dressy enough), department formal clutch — **audit hardware metal**  
- Approx. price range: $80–$300  
- Where to buy: brand sites  

## Styling & Pairings
- Pairs well with: all Soft Formal dresses and suit  
- Occasion: Soft Formal only — not everyday errands  
- Shoe pairings: N/A  

## Starlink One Pairing
- Hand/wrist visibility: N/A  
- Hardware finish: **silver / gunmetal only** — hard reject gold evening defaults  
- Ring echo: optional tiny chain-link detail; keep scale small  

## Chaos Incorporated Notes
- Aesthetic tie-in: silver discipline at formal scale — most evening bags default gold; walking away is the brand test.  
- Modifications wanted: chain detachable for true clutch moments.  
- Care instructions: wipe clean; store stuffed lightly.
